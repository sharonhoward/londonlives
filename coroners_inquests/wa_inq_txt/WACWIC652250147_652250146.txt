 An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields Bridewell in the Parish of St. Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty eighth day of March in the twenty fifth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Wheeler a Prisoner then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Seers , Anthony Fry , James Bean Joseph Williams , John Brady , Robert Bellamy William Fisher , James Daniels , John Phitheon Richard Richardson , Thomas Beard and Charles Carey good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Thomas Wheeler being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields Bridewell in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, on the said Twenty eighth day of March in the Year aforesaid, in Tothill Fields Bridewell aforesaid, departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural Way. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Samuel Seers Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

P Robert Foreman See
