City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to Wit 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church St. Peter Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty Second day of February in the Seventeenth Year of the Regin of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Samuel Hyde then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Charles Swindin , Edward Goodwin , John Thane , Richard Pitt , Henry Lay , Solomon Smith , Lawrence Long , David Niven , James Smart , Frederick Bergstorf , Gideon Saint and Paul Carr good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Samuel Hyde came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Samuel Hyde not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but lunatick and distracted, on the Twenty second day of February in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, one End of a certain piece of small Cord unto an Iron Hook fastened into a Wooden Beam in the Back Kitchen within the Dwelling House of Henry Beerling situate in Gerrard Street in the said Parish Liberty and County, and the other End thereof about his own Neck did fix tye and fasten, and therewith did then and there hang Suffocate and Strangle himself, of which said Hanging Suffocation and Strangling he the said Samuel Hyde then and there died. And so Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Samuel Hyde not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted in Manner and by the means aforesaid, did kill himself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Charles Swind in Foreman of the said Jurors, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Chas. Swindin Foreman
