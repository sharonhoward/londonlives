City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex } to wit  

 An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Knightsbridge in the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the Eighteenth day of February in the twenty sixth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Benjamin Allen  Esqr . then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Nicholas Osborne , Ralph Wills , John Colland , Thomas Morley William Claverley  Thomas Smith  John Vaughan  John Townley John Thomas  Joseph Thornley , William Westby  Henry Bowen and William Hichelwood good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charge to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Benjamin Allen came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Benjamin Allen not being of sound Mind Momory and Understanding but lunatic and Distracted on the said Eighteenth Day of February in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid, within the Liberty and County aforesaid, into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park , did Cast and throw himself by means of which said Casting and throwing he the said Benjamin Allen in the Water of the said River, was then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning, he the said Benjamin Allen then and there died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid, do say, that the said Benjamin Allen in Manner and by the Mean aforesaid not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted did drown and hill himself

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Nicholas Osborne  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the Rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place above mentioned

Tho Prichard Coroner

Nicholas Osborne Foreman
