City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty third day of September in the Twenty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Edward Pryce then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Joseph Jarvis , Samuel Palmer , John Peters , William Cornock William Wheeler , Richard Starks , Alexander Storey , William Hughes , Henry Dixon , John Crook , Mark Hildroth , Joseph Cooper , Philip Harper , Robert, Turnbull , Samuel Ewon  William Jeffreys , and Richard Hudson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Edward Pryce came to h is Death, do upon their oath say That the said Edward Pryce being a Prisoner in the Dwelling House of Thomas West a Sheriffs Officer situate in Berwick Street in the Parish aforesaid in the Liberty and County aforesaid on the Twenty second day of September in the Year aforesaid Departed this Life in a Natural way and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place first above Mentioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Jos Jarvis Foreman
