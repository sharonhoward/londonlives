City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex . } to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixteenth day of November in the Thirty Second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Robert Grierson then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of Henry Slack , William Prichard , Henry Witear , Alexander Burnie , William Dukeus , Henry Clunn , William Smith Thomas Dodswell , John Chilcott , George Tupman , John Watts and William Goff good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Robert Grierson came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say,  That on the Sixteenth day of November in the Year aforesaid the said Robert Grierson was found drowned in a certain Bason or Water in the Middle Park within the Liberty, and County aforesaid that no Marks of Violence appeared on the Body and that the said Robert Grierson Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune was then and there drowned and suffocated And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the Robert Grierson in manner and by the means aforesaid Accidentally casually and by Misfortune came to his Death and not otherwise

It Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Henry Slack  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hand and seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

Henry Slack Foreman
