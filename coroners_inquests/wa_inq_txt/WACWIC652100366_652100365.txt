City & Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to Wit 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovererign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Thirtieth day of Jul y in the Tenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Mary Channon then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Gill Robert Salmon , Lewis Graves , William Holmes , James Yates , Francis Hall , John Honor , Robert Croper , Thomas Halton , John Crather , Joseph Hibb , William Racass , Thomas Turner , James Marshal , John Price Thomas Middleton , and James Mc Cord good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by that what Means the said Mary Channon came to h er Death do upon their Oath say,

That the said Mary Channon not being of saint Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted on the Twenty eighth day of July in the Ten Aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid one and of a certain peice of Stuff or Quarter unto a Wood on Pig fastened into the wainsed in the Lodging Room of her the said Mary Channon in the Dwelling House of her Master Samuel King  Victualler in Bedford bury suitable and being in the said Parish and Liberty, and the other and thereof about her own Rock did five tye and fasten, and therewith did then and there Hang In frate and stangle herself, of which said Hanging Suffocation and Strangling, she the said Mary Channon then and then Did And do the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid So say, That the said Mary Channon not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted in manner and by the means aforesaid did Kill herself. In witness where of as well the said Coronor as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place above mentioned

Tho Prickard Coronor

James Gill Foreman
