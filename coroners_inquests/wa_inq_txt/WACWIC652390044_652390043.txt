City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex ,} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of Dixon Strachan the Sign of the black Horse orange Court  Swallow Street in the parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the fifth day of February 1799 in the thirty ninth- Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland King, Defendder of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Robert Stapleton then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Robert Stapleton came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Robert Stapleton not being of sound Mind Memory and understanding but lunatic and distracted on the thirty first day day of December in the Year of our Lord one thousand seven hundred and ninety eight at the Parish & in the Liberty aforesaid a great Quantity of Aqua for tis, being adeadly poison did then & there take drink & swallow down by means whereof he the said Robert Stapleton became then & there sick & distempered in her Body & of the Poison aforesaid & of the Sickness & Distember thereby occasioned from the said thirty first day of December until the third day of February in the year aforesaid at the Parish & in the Liberty aforesaid did languish and languishing did time until on which said third day of February in the Year aforesaid at the parish & in the liberty aforesaid she the said Robert Stapleton of the Poison aforesaid & of the Sickness & Distemper occasioned thereby did die. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their oath aforesaid do say that the sd Robert Stapleton not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatic and distracted in manner and by the means aforesaid did poison and kill himself

 IN WITNESS whereof, as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors, have to this Inqusition set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place first above written.

Anthy. Gell Coroner } Francis Richards Edwd Lloyd Thomas John Richardson George Dowie Joseph Quetch
