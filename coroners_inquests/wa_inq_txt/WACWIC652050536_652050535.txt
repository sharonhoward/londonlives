City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex , to Wit}  An Inquisition Indented taken for Our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Margaret in the City of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster in the County of Middlesex the Twenty sixth day of October in the Sixth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defen -der of the Faith and so forth, before Thomas Prickard Gentleman Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty on view of the Body of Thomas Walker then and there lying Dead upon the Oath of James Affleck , Charles Taylor  Henry Friday , James Donn  William Huxtable  John Derippi  Miles Wells , Solomon Morgan , David Williams , Thomas Phillips , Thomas Trubshaw , William Shakleford , Joseph Faweet , Wm Wilkinson , William Thomas , Wm. Fidoe , and Thomas Moses , good and lawfull Men of the said Liberty duly chosen, who being then and there duly Sworn and Charged to inquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what means the said Thomas Walker Came to his Death do upon their Oath say that the said Thomas Walker a Lighterman on the Twenty sixth day of October in the Year aforesaid being on Board of a certain Barge loaden with Sea Coals, and Rowing the said Barge in and upon the River Thomas in the Parish aforesaid within the City Liberty and County aforesaid; It so happened that the said Thomas Walker Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune did then and there fall out of and from the loaded Barge into the said River Thomas, and in the Waters thereof was then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said Thomas Walker then and there died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid, do say, that the said Thomas Walker in Manner and by the Means aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his Death and not otherwise, In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said James Affleck Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first above written

Thos Prickard Coroner

James Affleck
