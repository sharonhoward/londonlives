City & Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit,   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the king at the Parish of Saint James, Westminster , within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty third of August 1792 in the thirty second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq.  Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Goodwin then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of Samuel Lee , Peter Pike , James Holt , James Branmer , John Evans , Thomas Phillips , John Cock , William Browm , Thomas Haworth , Thomas Oxford , John Nueman , Henry Hall , Theophilus, Mack Cartney , Hugh Anderson , Edmund Hlliar , & Horatios Robson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by that what Means the said John Goodwin came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, that the said John Goodwin on the twenty second day of August in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid It so happened that he the said John Goodwin was then and there suddenly seized with a Fit, and by the Violence thereof he the said John Goodwin then and there fell backwards upon and against the stairs in the Dwelling House of Hanna Hilliard at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid, what he the said John Goodwin lodged and that he the said John Goodwin departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural way To Wit of the violent Fit aforesaid. And no Marks of Violence and Bruises appeared on the Body of him said John Goodwin . And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said John Goodwin in the manner and by the means aforesaid, came to his Death and not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coronor as the said Samuel Lee Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and place above written

Anthy Gell Coroner

Samuel Lee Foreman
