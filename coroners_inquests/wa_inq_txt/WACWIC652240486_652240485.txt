City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixth day of September in the Twenty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the ing for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Sparkes Barber then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Price ; Richard Ladd , Thomas Mann , Thomas Rowel James Douglas  Michael Palmer , Robert Hammond , Thomas Eanes James Munday , John Burgan  John Roebuck , John Maxwell , John Slappey , and Peter Bence good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Sparkes Barber came to h is Death, do upon their oath say That the said John Sparkes Barber not being of Sound Mind Memory and understanding but Lunatic and Distracted in the Fourth day of September in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid to wit at a certain peice of Water called the Serpentine River in Hyde Park did cast and row himself into the said he the said John Sparkes Barber in the said Water was suffocated and Drowned of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said John Sparkes Barber then and there instanlly Died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said John Sparkes Barber not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted in manner and by the means aforesaid did Kill himself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

James Price Foreman
