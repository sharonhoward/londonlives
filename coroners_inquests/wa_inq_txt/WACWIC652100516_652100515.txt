City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the the King, at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Ninth day of November in the Eleventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Bonner then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Samuel Roome , Edward Williams , William Nicoll , William Nicholson  George Smith , James Barnett , John Hawes , Thomas Maddox , John Hughes , Thomas Wells , Lewis Nillion , John Ward , George Martin John Cage , John Williams and Thomas Heaton good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when; how, and by what Means the said Thomas Bonner came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Thomas Bonner on the Nineteenth day of October in the Year aforesaid, going into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park , in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, there to bath himself, It so happened that Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune, he the said Thomas Bonner , was in the Waters of the said River then and there suffocated and Drowned, of which said suffocation and Drowning he the said Thomas Bonner then and there instantly died: and so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid, do say, that the said Thomas Bonner , in manner and by the Means aforesaid, Accidentally Casually and by misfortune came to his Death, and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Samuel Roome Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first abovewritten

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Saml. Roome Foreman
