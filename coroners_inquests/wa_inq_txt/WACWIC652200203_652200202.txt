City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at The Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the fourth day of May in the Twentieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Painter then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Jump , John Marsh , William Wright , John Woodward Roger Gill , William Walker , John Healy , Thomas Wilton , William Kendall , Joseph Ball , Joseph Richardson , Roger Segar and William Stonell good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what means the said Thomas Painter came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Thomas Painter , not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted on the Third day of May in the year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid one End of a certain Piece of small Cord unto the Iron Latch of the Kitchen Door in the dwelling House of Thomas Blake situate and being in the said Parish Liberty and County and the other End thereof about his own neck did fix tye and fasten, and therewith did then and then hang suffocate and strangle himself, of which said Hanging suffocation and Strangling he the said Thomas Painter then and there died. And so the Juroros aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Thomas Painter not being of sound Mind Memory and understanding but lunatick and distracted in manner and by the Means aforesaid did kill himself. In Withness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said William Jump Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and seals the Day year and Place above written

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Wm Jump Foreman}
