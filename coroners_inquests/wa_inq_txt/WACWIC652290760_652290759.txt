City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty ninth day of December in the Thirtieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Robert Ford then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Peter Syson , Thomas Field , John Wholen , Thomas Whiting , Benjamin Uphill , Joseph Exer , George Cape Thomas Grona , Hugh Mouro , Richard Tanner , Thomas Storer and Peter Copland good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Robert Ford came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Robert Ford not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and distracted on the Twenty Eighth day of December in the Year aforesaid into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park in the said Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid did cast and throw himself, by Means of which said casting and throwing he the said Robert Ford in the Water of the said River was then and there suffocated and Drowned of which said suffocation and Drowning he the said Robert Ford then and there died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Robert Ford in manner and by the means aforesaid not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding by Lunatic and distracted did kill himself.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Peter Syson Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Peter Syson Foreman
