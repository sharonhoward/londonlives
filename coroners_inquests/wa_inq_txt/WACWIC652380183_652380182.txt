City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex ,}  to wit,

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the House of Mr. Masters the Sign of the Kings head near Temple Bar in the Parish of St. Clements within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirty first day of March 1798 in the thirty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Kanes Ford then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Kanes Ford came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Kanes Ford on the twenty thirtieth day of March in the year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid to near Temple Bar aforesaid died by the visitation of God in a natural way To Wit of an Apoplexy and not otherwise

IN WITNESS whereof, as well the said Coroner as the Jurors, have to this Inqusition set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place first above written.

Anthy Gell Coroner }

Rd. Prior Jas Falkner Thos. Fisher John Langfield Jno Jms. Pollard
