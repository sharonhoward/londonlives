City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit. An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty sixth day of December in the Eleventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Jones then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Richard Williams , Henry Roberts , John Smith , James Hesketh  John Ballisatt , Henry Cook , Thomas Coe , James Barnett , Samuel Simmons  Thomas Rasin , Charles Howard  John Andrew Parazzo , Cuthbert Smith , Henry Pitt , and Thomas Porter good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King , when, how, and by what Means the said Thomas Jones came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Thomas Jones on the Twenty fourth day of December in the Year aforesaid, being at Work at the Water Works upon the River in a Place called Abery Farm, in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, It so happened that the said Thomas Jones then and there Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune fell into the said River, and in the Waters thereof was suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said Thomas Jones then and there died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Thomas Jones in Manner and by the Means aforesaid, Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his death, not otherwise. In Witness whereof as the said Coroner, as the said Richard William Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

Richd Willians Foreman
