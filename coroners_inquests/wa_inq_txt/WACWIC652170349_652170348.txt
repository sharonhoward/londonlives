City of Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Thirty first day of July in the Seventeenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Sarah Fenwick then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Thomas Holland John Smith , Edward Richardson , Richard Kent , John Henesey , George Russell , Jonathan Griffiths , Joseph Allen , John Armstrong , John De Jersey , William Yalden , David Allen Francis Whaley , John Elyas , William Clark , John Woodcock , and Henry Banting good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Sarah Fenwick came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Sarah Fenwick not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted on the Thirtieth day of July in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid in the Liberty and County aforesaid, to wit in a certain River called the Thames near to Manchester Stairs by Westminster Bridge , did Cast and throw herself by means of which casting and throwing she the said Sarah Fenwick in the Waters of the said River was then and there Suffocated and Drowned of which said Suffocation and Drowning she the said Sarah Fenwick then and there instantly Died. And so the Jurors aforesaid on their Oath aforesaid do say That the Said Sarah Fenwick not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted in manner and by the means aforesaid did Kill herself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner . Thos Holland foreman
