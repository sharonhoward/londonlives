City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixteenth day of December in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Edward Hodgkinson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Thomas Sime , John Ireland , Thomas Wright Philip Adams , Joseph Barber , Stephen Tilbury , James Lowndes Jacob Dawson , James Diddep , Thomas Nesbitt , John Ward , William Jones and John Eddy good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Edward Hodgkinson came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That on the fifteenth day of December in the Year aforesaid the said Edward Hodgkinson a Sawyer being in a certain Saw Pitt situate in Bull Head Yard in the said Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty and County aforesaid and that the said Edward Hodgkinson removed a certain Transom upon which a large piece of Mahogany was lying and that one end of the said piece of Mahogany then and there fell down upon the Head of the said Edward Hodgkinson by reason whereof he the said Edward Hodgkinson then and there Accidentally Casually and by misfortune removed one mortal Bruise and Fracture in and upon the back part of the Head of him the said Edward Hodgkinson of which said mortal Bruise ad Fracture he the said Edward Hodgkinson then and there instantly die. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Thomas Sime Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellow in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard  Coroner Thos Sime Foreman
