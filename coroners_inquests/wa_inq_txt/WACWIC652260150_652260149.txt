City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex }to wit 

 An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Martin on the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter , Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty seventh day of February in the twenty sixth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland. King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Nathaniel Morgan then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Jones  James Barteley , John Clule , Edward Mears . Thomas Mc Claland , John Budd . John Arnold Thomas Cousins  Thomas Peat  Joseph Taylor   John Byram , James Rayor , Charles Humpris  John Smith and William Collinson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Nathaniel Morgan came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Nathaniel Morgan and being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but Lunatick and Distracted on the twenty fifth day of February in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, One in the Lodging Room or Apartment of him the said Nathaniel Morgan in the Dwelling House of Thomas Read , situate in Orange Street in the said Parish Liberty and County and the other End there of about his own Neck did fix tie and faster and there with did then and there Lang Suffocate an Strangle himself of which said hanging Suffocation and Strangling He the said Nathaniel Morgan Oath aforesaid do say that the said Nathaniel Morgan not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but lunatick and distracted in Manner and by the Means aforesaid did hill himself

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the William Jones  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place above mentioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

W Jones Foreman
