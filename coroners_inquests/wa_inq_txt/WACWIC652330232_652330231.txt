City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex .} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Dwelling House of William Hatchett in Eaton Street  Pimlico , Parish of St. George Hanover Square  Sign of the Shakespear within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty seventh day of April 1793 in the thirty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Jonathan Estall an Infant then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are hereunder written and Seals affixed good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire of our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Jonathan Estall came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Jonathan Estall on the twenty sixth day of April in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid To Wit in or near pimlies Wharf there being at play with his Brother it so happened that accidentally, casually and by Misfortune he the said Jonathan Estall rolled from off the Bank Side into the Water, by meant whereof, he the said Jonathan Estall then & there was suffocated and drowned Of which said Suffocation & Drowning he the said Jonathan Estall then & there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their both aforesaid, do say, that the said Jonathan Estall in manner and by the means aforesaid accidentally casually & by Misfortune came to his Death & act otherwise In Witness whereof aswell the said Coroner as the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their hands & Seals the Day, Year, & place first above written.

Anthy Gell Coroner }

Wm. Judson Foreman}
