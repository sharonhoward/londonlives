City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex . to wit. An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Clement Danes within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty eighth day of April in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Catherine Foster then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of David Morris , John Stephens , William Bethall , John Line , James Mc. Farlane , Richard Chalener , John Cay , Daniel Clancy , Stephen Stephens , Jacob Beuler , John Judgson and Thomas Jones good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Catherine Foster came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Catherine Foster Wife of Isaac Foster (suspected to have been lately murthered) on the twenty sixth day of April in the year aforesaid at the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes within the Liberty and County aforesaid was suffocated and strangled by excessive Drinking and not by any Violence a Injury from the said Isaac Foster her Husband or any other Person to the Knowledge of the said Jurors.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner , as the said David Morris Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

David Morris Foreman
