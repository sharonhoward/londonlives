City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition, Indented, for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter. Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Thirty first day of May in the twenty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Benjamin Lacey then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Savage , Edward Edwards , Henry Jay , James Cowan , Peter Binell , Thomas Brown  William Forrester , Robert Thropp Thomas Burrage , Joseph Newland , James Cotes , Francis Jackson  Edward Price Edward Pett and Samuel Collett good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Benjamin Lacey came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Benjamin Lacey , not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted, on the Thirtieth day of May in the Year aforesaid one End of a certain Peace of Cord unto a den Noual in the Hayloft in a Stable Yard Situate in Wind mill Street in the said Parish Liberty and County and the other End thereof about his own Neck did fix tye and fasten; and therewith did then and there Hang suffocated and Strangle himself, of which said Hanging Suffocation and Strangling he the said Benjamin Lacey then and there died. And to the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Benjamin Lacey not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted in Manner and by the Means aforesaid, did kill himself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Savage  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

James Savage Foreman
