Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Jean Frampton then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly Chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Jean Frampton came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Sean Frampton on the eleventh day of June in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid To wit at the Dwelling House of William Taylor no 5 in Bird street died by the Visitation of God in a natural way to wit of a Fit and not otherwise

 IN WITNESS whereof, as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors, have to this Inqusition set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place first above written.

Anthy. Gell Coroner }
