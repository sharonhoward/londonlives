City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Knightsbridge in the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty third day of June in the twenty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Callochin then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Ralph Mills , George Puffer , John Jefferies , James Dowdle , Stephen Allen , Thomas Barber , Henry List , Richard Beadle , John Bradshaw , James Wolley , Thomas Morley and James Pagan good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Callochin came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say , That the said John Collachin, not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but lunatick and distracted on the Twenty second day of June in the Year aforesaid, into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, did cast and throw himself, by Means of which said Casting and Throwing he the said John Callochin in the Water of the said River was then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said John Callochin then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say That the said John Callochin in Manner and by the Means aforesaid, not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted did drown and kill himself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Ralph Mills  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner Ralph Mills Foreman
