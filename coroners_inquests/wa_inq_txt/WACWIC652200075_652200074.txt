City and Liberty of Westminster  in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  

 An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields  Bridewell in the Parish of St. Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eighth day of February in the Twentieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William Kitchenman then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Jabez Price William Haywood , Samuel Highley , Thomas Stanley , James Blackgrove , John Lane , Thomas Wilkinson , Richard Hollis , George Tyler , Jacob Powell ,  James Wood and John Chewter good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said William Kitchenman came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said William Kitchenman a Prisoner in Tothill Fields  Bridewell situate in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, on the Fifth day of February in the Year aforsaid, at Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid he the said William Kitchenman departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a Natural Way, to Wit of a Fever, and not otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Jabez Price Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

Jabez Price Foreman
