City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the eighth day of January in the twenty Eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great- Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William whose Surname is not known then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Edward Howis . Joseph Allison , Thomas Hannon Robert Clark , James Walker , John Field , Lewis Dupis Joseph Harris , James Hicks , James Ridgway Richard Lloyd  William Filler Richard Evans & John Orger good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said William whose Surname is not known came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said William whose Surname is not known (suspected to have been murdered) on the Fifteenth day of December in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of St. James within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life by excessive Orinking and not from any hurt or violence done or committed by Joseph Fisher or any other Person whatsoever to the Knowledge of the said Jurors

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Edward How is Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Ed Howis Foreman
