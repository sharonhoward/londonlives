City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at Tothill Fields  Bridewell situate in the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westmr. within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty fourth day of December in the Eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Anne Cordinier a Prisoner then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Curtis , William Lowdie , William Cranwill , Amos Charles , John Simons , John Harper , John Groumant , Alexander Trotter , Richard Caves , George Orman  William Lockie and John Blatch good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Anne Cordinier came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say , That the said Anne Cordinier being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid, in the said Parish of St. Margaret within the City Liberty and County aforesaid, on the Twenty= =third day of December in the Year aforesaid, at Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid, departed this Life, by the Visitation of God, in a natural Way, to Wit, of a Fever, and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said John Curtis , Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

John Curtis Foreman
