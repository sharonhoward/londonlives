City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex .} to wit   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Margaret Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Fourth day of May in the thirty second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq.  Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William Smith then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of George Puffer , Edward Carey , William Ellis , James Brown , John Beedell , Henry Dyor , James Pagen , Thomas Shirland , William Pinner Joseph Mathew , John Glover , & John Jagg good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly Sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said  William Smith came to his Death, do upon their Oath say that the said William Smith not being of sound Mind, Memory and Understanding but lunatic & distracted on the third day of May in the Year aforesaid in the Parish & in the Liberty aforesaid to wit into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park there did cast & throw himself by Means of which said Casting and throwing he the said williams Smith in the waters of the said Rivers was then and there suffocated & drowned of which said suffocation and Drowning be the said William Smith then and there instantly died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their aforesaid, do say that the said William Smith in manner & by means aforesaid not being of sound Mind, Memory and understanding but lunatic and distracted did drown and kill himself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said George Puffer , Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their hands & Seals the Day, Year and Place abovementioned.

Anthy Gell Coroner Geo Puffer Foreman
