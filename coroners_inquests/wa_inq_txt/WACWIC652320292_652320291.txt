City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .}  to wit

 An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint James Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eleventh day of May in the thirty second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq.  Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Gilbert Togwell then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of  William Giles , John Brock  William Wilson , Benjamin Bishop , Geary Reid  John Carpenter , James Maud , John Mosey , Samuel Heayes  Charles Legh  James cook , Henry white , Richard Walters , John Seete , Joseph Davison , Thomas Smith and George Dalton good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen,who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said  John Gilbert Togwell came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said John Gilbert Togwell on the Ninth day of May in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid in the Liberty aforesaid, being cleaning the parlour Windows of the House of the Right Honourable Lord viscount Countenay in Grosvenor Square it so happened that he the said John Gilbert Togwell did then and there accidentally casually, and by Misfortune fell from one of the said Parlour Windows upon the stone Pavement of the Area belonging to the said House & by means there of did then and there accidentally, casually, and by Misfortune receive one Mortal Bruise & Wound on his Threat & a Violent Concussion of the Brain of which said Mortal Bruise & Wound, and Violent Concussion of the Brain as aforesaid he the said John Gilbert Togwell from the said Ninth day of May in the Year aforesaid untill the tenth day of the same Month in the same year at the Parish aforesaid did languish and languishing did live on which said tenth day of August in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid to Wit in Lucas Court there, he the said John Gilbert Togwell of the Mortal Bruise & wound and violent Concussion aforesaid did die And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their oath aforesaid do say that the said John Gilbert Togwell in Manner and by the means aforesaid accidentally, casually and by Mis fortune came to his Death and not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said William Giles  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals, the Day Year & Place abovementioned

Anthony Gell Coroner

Willm Giles Foreman
