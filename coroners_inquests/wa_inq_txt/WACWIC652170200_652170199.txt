City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty Second day of April in the Seventeenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great- Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Lansbury then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William House , William Prosser , Francis Gendre  William Corderoy William Adams , John Bishop , James Doyle , James Church William Jones , John Stephenson , Tobias Williams , Henry Sawyer and Peter Conolly good and Lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Lansbury came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said John Lansbury, (suspected to have been Murdered) on the Twenty first Day of the April in the Year aforesaid being Acting in a certain Puppet show in Panton Street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid and then and there died suddenly by the Visitation of God in a Natural, Way, and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Th House Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Wm. House Foreman
