City & Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex . } to Wit 

An Inquitition Indented, taken for our Sovererign Lord the king at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the Counrty of Middlesex , the First day of March in the Twenty Sixth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Man unknown then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Ward , John Baker , Alexander Caterson , John Williams , Henry Mc. Laughlin , John Bedford . Thomas Henderson , Edward Davis , George Martin . John Dallow , John Wooden , and Richard Exley good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when how and by that what Means the said Man Unknown came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Man whose Name is Unknown on the Twenty eighth day of February in the Year aforesaid was found dead in a certain saw pit in the Yard of John Kennett Coachmaker Situate in New Bond Street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That the said Man unknown Accidentially Casually and by Misforutne fell into the said Sawport, by the Violence of Which said fall, the Neck of him the said Man unknown was then and then broke and dislocated of which said breaking and Dislocation he the said Man unknown then and there said. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Man unknown in Manner and by the Means aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune, came to his Death.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Joseph Ward  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his fellows in their presence have to this Inquitition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place above Mentioned.

Tho Prickard Coroner

Josh Ward Foreman
