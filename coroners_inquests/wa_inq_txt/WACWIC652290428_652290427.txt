City of Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex . } to wit.

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of St. Anne within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty seventh day of July in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on on View of the Body of Anthony Dupny then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Potier , William Mc. Hewan , John Ready , William Ballistone , Thomas Pratbernon , Frederick Heerlin , William Duncan Alexander Kinlock , Alexander Callum , John Richter , Lous Buscarlet James Warren , William Patterson , William Duff , Thomas oliver , Edward Good child , John Sell , Peter Bayonds , Paul Lemaitre , John Brown , and Thomas Sarson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Anthony Dupny (Suspected came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Anthony Dupuy (suspected to have been Murthered) on the Twenty sixth day of July in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural Way, and not by any Violence or Injury done or committed by any Person whatsoever to the Knowledge of the said Jurors.

In Witness whereof as well the said Corner as the said William Potier Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence home to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Wm. Posier Foreman
