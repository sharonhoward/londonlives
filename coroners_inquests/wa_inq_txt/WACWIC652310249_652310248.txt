City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter , Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixth day of June in the Thirty first Year of the Reign on our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great - Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and So forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our Said Lord the King for the Said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Fuller an Infant then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of Richard Hughes , Walter Belcher , Samuel Sprang Richard Smith , John, Gaywood , James Smith , John Minnitt , Joseph Hickinbottom , William Auld , Dugal Anderson , John Unthank and James Baker good and lawful Men of the Said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly Sworn and charged to enquire our Said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Fuller came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say , That on the fourth day of June in the Year aforesaid the said, John Fuller the Infant being at Play, in the Garden of Adam Fuller his Father Situate in the said Parish of St George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid It so happened that he said John Fuller the Infant Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune fell into a certain Pond in the said Garden and then and there was Drowned and suffocated of which said drowning and Suffocation he the said John Fuller then and there died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their oath aforesaid do say that the said John Fuller the Infant in Manner and by the means aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to this Death and not otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Richard Hughes Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho Prickard Coroner

Richd Hughes Foreman
