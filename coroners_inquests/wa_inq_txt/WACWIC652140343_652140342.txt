City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex }to wit 

In Inquisition Indented taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of St. Anne within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Colleigate Church of St. Peter Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the First day of August in the fourteenth fear of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Gracer of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the Saith and so faith Before Thomas Prickard Gentleman Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty on view of the Body of William Green then and there Lying Dead by the Oaths of Francis Deschamps , Hugh Thomas , John Fowler , John Meyer Thomas Bolton , Thomas Wright , Robert Jones , Thomas Clay , David Williams , John Osborn , Jeremiah Freeman , George Streeting and Ralph Gould good and Lawfull Men of the said Liberty duly chosen, who being then and there duly Sworn and charged to Enquire for our said Lord the King when how and means the said William Green came to his Death, Do upon their Oath say, That the said William Green on the Thirty first day of July in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish of St Margaret within the Liberty and County aforesaid, going into the River Thomas, to Bath and Wash himself, It so happened that the said William Green Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune in the Water of the said Richer was then and there Suffocated and Drowned, which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said William Green then and there said. In Witness  whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Francis Deschamps Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho Prickard Corner

Francis Deschamps Foreman
