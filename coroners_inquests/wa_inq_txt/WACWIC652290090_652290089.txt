City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty eighth day of January in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Edward Mortimer then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Dedrick Smith , William Mc. Kowen , George Ainsworth William Wright , Fredrick Herbin , George Welshire Thomas Wood , John Duns , John Simmons , Thomas Unit , John Newfer , John Swan , John Greenland , Stephen La dere and George Mamson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Edward Mortimer came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Edward Mortimer on the twenty seventh day of January in the Year aforesaid being on the Foot way before the Door of Jeremiah Freeman situate in Chapel Street in the said Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty and County aforesaid, and that the said Edward Mortimer then and there in a Fit fell down upon the Stone pavement in the said Street, by means whereof the said Edward Mortimer did then and there Accidentally, Casually and by Misfortune receive a mortal Wound and Fracture in and upon the back part of his Head of which said mortal wound and Fracture he the said Edward Mortimer then and there instantly died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Edward Mortimer in manner and by the means aforesaid Accidentally Causally, and by misfortune came to this Death and not otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner , as the said Dedrick Smith Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner Dedrick Smith Foreman
