City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty third day of June in the twenty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Jonas Broad then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Bowden Brookes , John Budd , Alexander Martin John Strainor , Robert Stancy , John Gwillim , Arthur Goodwin , Gotlied Fribourg , John Oldham , David Clarke John Pugh , Thomas Myatt , Joseph Hobbs , John Deluze , John Kinlow and John Parazzo , good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Jonas Broad came to h is. Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said James Broad (suspected to have been lately Murdered) on the Twenty first day of June in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life, by the Visitation of God, in a Natural Way, to Wit, of an Inflammation in his Lungs, and not by any hurt or Injury from any Person whatever. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Bowden Brookes  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovewritten

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

Bowden Brookes Foreman
