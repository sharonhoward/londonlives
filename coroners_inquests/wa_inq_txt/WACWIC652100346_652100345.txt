City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex  } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty fourth day of July in the Tenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth; before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Hannah Lowrey then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Richard Burton Nathaniel Pearce , Robert Holling , John Seate , Nathaniel Silverthane , William Gibbs , Richard Owen William Baker , Joseph Halsey , Alexander Frier , Robert Layer , and Benjamin Talbut good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Hannah Lowrey came to her Death, do upon their Oath say

 That the said Hannah Lowrey not being of lound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted on the Twenty third day of July in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid to wit into a certain Water near to Chelsea Water Works did last and throw herself by means of which said lasting and throwing, she the said Hannah Lowrey in the said Waters was then and there Suffocated and Drowned of which laid suffocation and Drowning she the said Hannah Lowrey then and there instantly Died, And to the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, That the said Hannah Lowrey in Manner and by the means aforesaid not being of loud Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted did Drown and Kill herself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard  Coroner .

Richad. Burton Foreman
