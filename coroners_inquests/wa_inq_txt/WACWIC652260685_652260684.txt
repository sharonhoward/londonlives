City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eighteenth day of October in the twenty sixth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Robert Green then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Jones , John Wilson , Thomas Turner , Peter Archer , John Bayne , William Mayne , Thomas Lambert , Thomas Latham . Samuel Tipping , Edward Turner , Samuel Hendrick , Mathew Ray . John Milton  Richard Newton , Thomas Barefoot , James Urquhart , William Shard , and Lawrance Duff good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Robert Green came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Robert Green (suspected to have been Poisoned) on the Seventeenth day of October in the Year aforesaid, was found dead at the Sign of the Northumberland Arms situate and being in the Parish aforesaid, in the Liberty and County aforesaid, That the said Robert Green departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a Natural Way, and not by any Violent Ways or Means whatsoever, to the Knowledge of the said Jurors

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner , as the said William Jones Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner William Jones Foreman
