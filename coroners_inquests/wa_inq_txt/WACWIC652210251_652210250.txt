City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Fifth day of June in the Twenty first Year of the Regin of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of James Cobb then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Cates Charles Thirkill , Thomas Gosheron , James Debaufre , Andrew Oliver , John Ellis , William Allcock , John Etorneau , Anthony Jourdan , Thomas Prudahon Jonathan Fenton , John Hardy , William Baldwin , and William Albrecht good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said James Cobb came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said James Cobb on the Fourth day of June in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid to wit at the Dwelling House of Mr. Joseph Dancock the Sign of the Green Man in little Bedford Street did then and there by the Visitation of God Dye a Natural Death to wit of a Fit of Epilepsy and not from any Injury or violence received from any Person or Persons whatsoever. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this, Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard  Coroner . John Cates Foreman
