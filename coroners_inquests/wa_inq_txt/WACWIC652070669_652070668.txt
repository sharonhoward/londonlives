City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Gatehouse Prison in the Parish of St. Margaret in the City of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty seventh day of November in the Eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Anne Du Commines then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Joseph Greive , Robert Bray , Michael Castledine , Thomas Hudson , Thomas Hackstall , William Almon , Robert Dunn , Richard Morrell Joseph Gilley , James Legg , George Fairbone and John Harkness good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Anne Du Commines came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say , That the said Anne Du Commines being a Prisoner in the Gate house Prison aforesaid in the Parish and in the City Liberty and County aforesaid, on the Twenty sixth day of November in the Year aforesaid, at the Gatehouse Prison aforesaid, departed this Life by the Visitation of God, in a natural Way, to Wit, of a Fever, and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Joseph Greive Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Jos. Greive Foreman
