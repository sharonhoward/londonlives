City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex } to wit,

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of John Whems  Sign of the Green Coat Boy near Tothill Fields Bridewell within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of August 1793 in the thirty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and So forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our Said Lord the King for the Said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Richard Duncalf  Prisoner  Tothill Field Bardwell then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jury whore Names are here under written and Seals affixed good and lawful Men of the Said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly Sworn ad charged to enquire for our Said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the Said Richard Duncalf came to h is Death, do upon their Oath Say that the Said Richard Duncalf being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid on the Said Seventh day of August in the Year aforesaid in Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid died by the Visitation of God in a natural way To Wit of a decline. In Witness whereof as well the Said Coroner as the Said Jurors here to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the Day Year & place first above written.

Anthy Gell Coroner }

Jams Mason }

Rhyness
