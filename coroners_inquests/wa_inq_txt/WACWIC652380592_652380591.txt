City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex ,} to wit

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of John Armstead the Sign of the George in Hollen Street in the Parish of St. Anne Soho within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirtieth day of August 1798 in the thirty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland Kings, Defender of the Faith, and So forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our Said Lord the King for the Said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of George Dicterich then and there Lying dead, upon the Oath of the Several Jurors whose Names are here under Written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the Said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly Sworn and charged to enquire for our Said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the Said George Dicterich came to h is Death, do upon their Oath Say that the Said George Dicterich on the twenty ninth day of August in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid To Wit in the Dwelling House of James Gosling in Wardour Street died, by the Visitation of God in a natural Way and not otherwise

 IN WITNESS whereof, as well the Said Coroner as the Jurors, have to this Inqusition Set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place Frist above Written.

Anthy Gell Coroner } John Myers Foreman Geo Hutchins Thos. Mitchell J Yard

Josh. Perry John Welsons George Hervitson Josh Jarmeain Saml. Jones Robt Cuthberton

Joseph Dobson
