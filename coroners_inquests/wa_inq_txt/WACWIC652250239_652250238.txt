City & Liberty of Westminster . in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields Bridewell in the Parish of St. Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter , Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Thirtieth day of April in the twenty fifth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King, for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Sarah Rawlinson a Prisoner then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Perryman  William Crawford , Richard Ralph William Cotterel , Thomas Edwards , John Pacey , James Hammet , Francis Norris , Thomas Scott , Thomas Pearce , William Whitworth and James Swann good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Sarah Rawlinson came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Sarah Rawlinson being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields Bridewell in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid at Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid, departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a Natural Way, to wit, of a Fever and Jaundice, and not otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner , as the said Robert Perryman  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho Prickard  Coroner .

Robt Perryman Foreman
