City of Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex . } to wit.

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of November in the Twenty Seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Elizabeth Moore then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Thomas George , Timothy Bennet , Richard Waddap , James Johnson Thomas Scott , Anthony Fabrey , Wiliiam Armstige , Frederick Duperrey William Boxter , Nathaniel Jones , Richard Hatch , and William Ashdon good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Elizabeth Moore came to her Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Elizabeth Moore not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and Distracted, on the Sixth day of November in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid to wit into the River Thames there did Cast and Throw herself by Means of which said Casting and Throwing the the Said Elizabeth Moore in the Waters of the said River was then and there Suffocated and Drowned of which said Suffocation and Drowning she the said Elizabeth Moore then and there instantly Died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say That the said Elizabeth Moore in manner and by the means aforesaid not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted did Drown and Kill herself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Thos George Foreman
