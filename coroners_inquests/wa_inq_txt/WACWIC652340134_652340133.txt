City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex } to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the House of Archibald Fairbairn Sign of the Coach & Horses in Marlbro Street  parish of St James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the Fourteenth day of March 1794 in the thirty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of a Female Child then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written and seals affixed Good and Lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly Chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Female Child came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the Said Female Child on the twelfth day of March in the year aforesaid at th Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid To Wit near the Work house was found dead That the said Female Child had no Marks of Violence on its Body but appeared to have been still boon Bat how and by what Means the said Female Child came there no Evidence there of appeared to the said Jurors In Witness whereof as well the said Covoner as the said Juron hane to this Inquisition set their hands & seals the day year & Place above written

Anthy Gell Coroner } Wm Walker Fore Man John Wilson Francis Stokes David Skinner Thos Croft

Henry Bevan John Davis P Hutcheman John Broach Joseph Frances James Davis

Chas. Riley Thomas Farm Edwd Harper
