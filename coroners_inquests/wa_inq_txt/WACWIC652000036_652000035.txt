Whitehall 

An Inquisition Indented and taken at the House known by the Name of the Barn in the Mouse within the Verge of His Make's Palace of Whitehall the Twenty eighth day of July in the thirty Fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Second by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the faith and so forth and in the Year of our Lord One Thousand Seven Hundred and Sixty . Before John Feary Coroner for His Majesty's Palace of Whitehall and the Verge of the same Upon a view of the Body of Ann Cox lying Dead within the said Verge by the Oaths of George Woodfall , John Lambert , William Blunt , Aaron young , Leonard Phillips , Nathaniel Hargrave , Richard Richardson , John Humphries , William Grinby , John Troy , Thomas Beeton , Samuel Blomfeild , John Hubbard , William Hannington , Patrick Cannon , Thomas Mountain , and Henry Timbrell Good and Lawfull Men residing and Dwelling within His Maties said Palace of Whitehall and the Verge thereof who being Sworn and Charged to Enquire for our Sovereign Lord the King how when where and in what manner the said Ann Cox come to her Death say upon their Oaths. That on Saturday the twenty Sixth day of July Instant the said Ann Cox being at a House adjoining to her Dwelling House scituate within in the Meuse within the Verge of His Majesty's Palace of Whitehall from the Visitation of God of a Suffocation occasioned by a sudden Just of Passion Died a Natural Death, And so the said Jurors upon this Oath, aforesaid do say that the said Ann Cox on the Day aforesaid in the year aforesaid within the Verge aforesaid in manner and form aforesaid by her Death came And not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the day year and at the place abovesaid

Jno. Feary  Coroner Pro. Tem

George Woodfall Foreman
