City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit

An Inquisition Indented taken for Our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden , within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the First day of March in the Thirteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the Faith and so forth before Thomas Prickard Gentleman Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, On View of the Body of John Hembrough then and there lying dead upon the Oath of Thomas Spencer , John Harden , James Greeves Christopher Carless  John Bovingdon , Henry Swanemeyer  James Wiley , John Bartley , Barnaby Gurney , Adam Bozamor , David William , James Ferrell , Henry Brough , Robert George and William Droffer , good and lawfull Mon of the said Liberty day Chosen who being then and there duly Sworn and Charged to inquire for our Said Lord the King, when, how and by what Means the said John Hembrough came to his Death do upon their Oath say That the said John Hombrough and two other Men, Namely, John Robertson and Richard Davis , on the Thirtieth day of January in the Year aforesaid with Force and Arms at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid in and upon one Peter Senhouse in the Peace of God and of our said Lord the King and in the Kings Highway then and there being feloniously did make an Assault, and him the said Peter Senhouse in Bodily fear and Danger of his life in the Kings Highway aforesaid then and there feloniously did put, with intent the Goods Chattels and Moinces of the said Peter Senhouse from the Person and against the Will of the said Peter Senhouse in the Kings Highway aforesaid then and there feloniously to steal take and carry away, against the Peace of our said Lord the King his Crown and Dignity; Whereupon the said Peter Senhouse then and there having a certain Pistol of the Nalue of five shilling in his right Hand loaded with Gunpowder and a leaden Bullet he the said Peter Senhouse the same Pistol to and against the said John Hembrough in Defence of himself and his Property did then and there lawfully and Justifiably shoot off and discharge, and him the said John Hembrough , with the leaden Bullet aforesaid so discharged from and out of the said Pistol by force of the Gun powder aforesaid, in and upon his Forehead over his left Eye did then and there strike wound and penetrate, Giving to the said John Hembrough then and there in and upon his said Forehead over his left Eye one mortal Wound and Fracture, of which said mortal Wound and Fracture h the said John Hembrough at the Parish last mentioned and also at the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent within the Liberty and County aforesaid, from the Said Thirtieth day of January in the Year aforesaid until the Twenty seventh day of February in the same Year, did languish and languishing did live, on which said Twenty seventh day of February in the Year aforesaid, the said John Hembrough at the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty and County aforesaid, of the said mortal Wound and Fracture did die. And that the said Peter Senhouse him the said John Hembrough in manner and form aforesaid in Defence of himself and his Property did lawfully and Justifiably kill and Slay. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Thomas Spencer Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first abovewritten

Tho Prickard  Coroner

Thos. Spencer Foreman
