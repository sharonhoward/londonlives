City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign, the King at the House of John Grant in Market Lane , Parish of St. James  Sign of the Cock within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirteenth day of May 1793 in the thirty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain,France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Mary Hefford then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are hereunder written and Seals affixed good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Mary Hefford came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, that the said Mary Hefford on the Eleventh day of May in the year aforesaid, at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid to Wit, at her Dwelling House, in Market Lane aforesaid, there being at the top of the said House, It so happened that accidentally causally and by Misfortune she the said Mary Hefford From off of the Top of the said House into the yard did fall by Means whereof, she the said Mary Hefford then and there received one Mortal Wound, on the Head of her the said Mary Hefford of which said Mortal wound , she the said Mary Hefford then and there instantly died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid, do say, that the said Mary Hefford , in manner and by the Means aforesaid accidentally casually & by Misfortune came to her Death and not otherwise, In witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the Day Year & place first above written.

Anthy. Gell Coroner }

Herry Wells Foreman Richd Evans

Thos Gourney Stephen Staintan John Bond
