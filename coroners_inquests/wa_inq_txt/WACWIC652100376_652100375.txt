City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex  } to wit. An Inquisicion Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirty first day of July in the Tenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth; before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Parker then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Anthony Ansell William Roberts , John Brooks , Samuel Mitchell , George Walls , Thomas Wheeler , Richard Poater William Toff , John Records , Nathaniel Scate , Robert Turnbull , and Samuel Everringham good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Thomas Parker came to his Death, do upon their Oath say

 That the said Thomas Parker on the Twenty ninth day of July in the Year aforesaid being Riding and Galloping of a certain Mare in a certain Place called Scotland Yard in the Parish of Sain Martin in the fields in the Liberty it to happended that accidentally casually and by Misfortune to the said Thomas Parker was then and there flung from the back of the said More by the Violence of her Motion and one of the fest of him the said Thomas Parker hanging in the Shrrup Lather fastened to the Body of the said Mare and the said Mare contuning going on full Speed he the said Thomas Parker was by reason thereof flung and best to and against several Posts in the Said Place called Scotland Yard by means whereof he the said Thomas Parker received divers Violent and Merlal Wounds and Fractares on the Head and Several other parts of the Body of him the said Thomas Parker , of which said Mortal wound and Fractares he the said Thomas Parker did langquish and languishing did Live for the Space of Three Hours on which said Tenty ninth day of July he the said Thomas Parker in the Westminster Hospital in James Street in the Parish of Saint margaret in the Liberty aforesaid of the said Mortal wounds and Fracturers did Dye And to the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that th said Thomas Parker a Manner and by the means aforesaid accidentally casually and by Misfortune come to his Death and not otherwise, and that the said Mare was Moving to the Death of the said Thomas Parker and is of the Value of Five Sillings and the Property and in the Possession of William Cook Head Porter to the Admirally or of his Assigns. In Witness whereof as well the said Corner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors house to this Inquisiton their Hands and leals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard  Coroner .

Anthony A lon Foreman
