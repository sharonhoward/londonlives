City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex ,} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of Thomas Rixon the Sign of the Harp in Carnaby Street parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter , Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the fourteenth day of November 1796 in the thirty Seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq. Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Robert Carnagy then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Robert Carngy came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Robert Carnagy not being of sound Mind Memory and understanding (being seized with a sudden Frenzy of the Brain) but lunatic and distracted on the twelfth day of November in the year aforesaid at the parish and in the Liberty aforesaid, one end of a certain piece of small Cord unto a Nail fastened in the upper part of the door way of the Coal Cellor belonging to the dwelling House of him the said Robert Carnagy Scituate in Carnaby Sheet aforesaid, and the other end thereof about his own teck did fix tye and fasten and there with did then and there hang suffocate and Strangle himself; Of which said Hanging Suffocation & Strangling he the said Robert Carnagy then and there instantly died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their oath aforesaid do say that the said Robert Carnagy not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatic and distracted in manner and by the Means aforesaid did kill himself

 IN WITNESS whereof, as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors, have to this Inqusition set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place first above written.

Anthy. Gell Coroner } Thos Oxford Foreman Stephen Penny James Halbeck Wm. Ramsay Richd Williams
