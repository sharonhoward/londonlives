City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the House of Wm. Hawkins Sign of the Red Lyon , Parliament Street Parish of St. Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty eighth day of January 1794: in the thirty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq. Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Sophia Poignand an Infant then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors where Names are here under written and seals affixed good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Sophia Poignand came to her Death, do upon their Oath say, that the said Sophia Poignand on the twenty fourth day of January in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid being alone in a certain room or Apartment in her Fathers House situate in Parliament Sheet It so happened that the Clothes which she the said Sophia Poignand then And on her Body accidentally casually and by Misfortune, took Fire by reason whereof and from the Smoke and Flame arising from the said Fire she the said Sophia Poignand was then and there suffocated and burnt: Of which said Suffocation & Burning she the said Sophia Poignand then and there shortly died: And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Sophia Poignand in manner and by the means aforesaid accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to her Death fact otherwise

In Witness here of as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors cause to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the day Year and Place above written

Anthy. Gell Coroner }

George Johnston Foreman Chas Iggulden John Eastwood

Wm. Johnson Matthew Turner William Thomas Thomas Prior
