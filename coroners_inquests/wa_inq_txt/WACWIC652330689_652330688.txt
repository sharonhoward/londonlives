City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of John Williams  Sign of the Cock & Horse  in Jermyn Street  parish of St. James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter the Collegiate Church of St. Peter,Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the fourth day of November 1793 in the thirty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Francis Smith then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the Several Jurors whose Names are hereunder written and Seals affixed good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, now, and by what Means the said Francis Smith came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Francis Smith on the third day of November in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid to wit at the Dwelling House of Joseph Micklewight of Saint James's Market died by the Visitation of God in a natural way, and not otherwise

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors came to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the day Year and Place first above written.

Anthy Gell Coroner Richd Beath Foreman John Bristie John Arnold Thos. Greenaway Geo. Michel

William Garden Stephen Staintan David Jones Richd Evans Tho John Fitley

Elizah Whitaker John Marsh Wm. Staines
