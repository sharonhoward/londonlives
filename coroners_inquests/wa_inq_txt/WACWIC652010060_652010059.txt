Westminster City and Liberty in the County of Middlesex } to wit An Inquisition Indented and taken at the Parish of St. Martin in the fields in the Liberty of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter at Westminster the Twenty fourth day of September in the First Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the faith and so forth, and in the Year of our Lord One Thousand seven hundred and sixty one . Before John Feary Coroner for our Sovereign Lord the King for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid Upon a View of the Body of a New Born Female Child then and there lying Dead by the Oaths of Constantine Tenlings , Robert Haynes , Thomas Stockdale , James Dixwell , Thomas Forshall , Jeremiah Bates , Francis Pitsalas  George Patterson , David Rose , Christian Henrechson , Edward Rostrand , George Scott Francis Benwill , Thomas Proist , William Carpenter , Richard Farguson , and Thomas Gardiner Good and Lawfull Men of the said City and Liberty who being Sworn and Charged to Enquire for our Sovereign Lord the King how when where and in what manner the said Female Child came to her Death, say upon their Oaths That Hester Rowden late of the said Parish in the Liberty and County aforesaid Singlewoman on the Twenty Frist day of September Instant in the Year aforesaid, being big with the said Female Child afterwards to wit, on the same day in the same Year at the Parish aforesaid in the Liberty and County aforesaid the said Female Child alone secretly from her Body by the Providence of God did being forth alive which said Female Child by the Laws and Customs of this Realm was a Bastard. And that the said Hester Rowden not having the Fear of God before her Eyes but moved and seduced by the Instigation of the Devil, afterwards to wit on the Same Day and in the same Year with Force and Arms at the Parish aforesaid in the Liberty and County aforesaid in and upon the said Female Bastard Child so Alive and in the Peace of God and of our said Lord the King then and there being Feloniously Wilfully and of her Malice fore thought did make an Assault, and that she the said Hester Rowden a certain String or Garter with both her Hands about the Neck of the said Female Bastard Child did fix round tye and fasten and the said Female Bastard Child there with then and there Feloniously Wilfully and of her Malice forethought did Choak Strangle, and Suffocate of which said Choaking strangling and Suffocating she the said Female Bastard Child then and there instantly Died. And so the said Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Hester Rowden her the said Female Bastard Child in manner and by the means aforesaid Feloniously Wilfully and of her Malice forethought did Kill and Murder against the Peace of our said Lord the King his Crown and Dignity And that the said Hester Rowden at the time of the doing and committing of the Felony and Murder aforesaid or at any time since had no Goods or Chattels Lands or Tenements within the said Liberty or elsewhere to the Knowledge or Notice of the, said Jurors. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the day Year and at the Place abovementioned.

Jno. Feary Coroner

Constantine Tenlings Foreman
