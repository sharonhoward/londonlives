An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Cecil Street House within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty third day of June in the 32d. Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq.  Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Gustavus Nicholas Frizell then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of James Birchall , Henry Thompson  Thomas Roe  John Dutton  Nathaniel Garden  James Bubb  John Charlton  William Morris  Robert Knife  John Fenkum William Barron  Thomas Stokes  Nathaniel Bone  Joseph Knight James Robinson  John Farsfield
