City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Nineteenth day of September in the Fourteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Jessamy Wade  then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Hammond , Samuel Harrison , James Aldridge , Issac Fenn , Robert Broom , Joseph Baker , Richard Terrey , Nicholas Clarke , Thomas Hill , Isaac Reddington , Thomas Beard , John Wheeler , Henry Godfrey , Thomas Martha and John Webb good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Jessamy Wade came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Jessamy Wade (Suspected to have been Murdered) on the Nineteenth day of September in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid, departed this life by the Visitation of God in a natural way, and not by any Injury from any Person whatsoever. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Robert Hammond Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Follows in their have to this inquisition at this Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first first abovewritten.

Thos Prickard  Coroner Robt. Hammond Foreman
