Westminster City and Liberty  in the County of Middlesex } to wit An Inquisition Indented and taken at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of Saint Peter at Westminster the Eighth day of January in the Second Year of the Reign of Our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain, France and Ireland King of the faith and so forth, and in the Year of our Lord One Thousand seven hundred and Sixty two. Before John Feary Coroner for our sovereign Lord the King for the City Liberty of Westminster aforesaid Upon a View of the Body of Christiana Randall then and there lying Dead by the Oaths of Thomas Barlow , John Fludgate , Robert Dickenson , James Cox , James Moett , Richard Evans , Caleb Atkinson John Corney , Thomas France , Joshua Taft , Christopher Morgan , William Linnell , Alexander Schooler , John Pratt and John Morris Good and Lawfull Men of the said City and Liberty who being Sworn and Charged to Inquire for our Sovereign Lord the King how when and in what manner the Said Christiana Randall came to her Death say upon their Oaths That the said Christiana Randall not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but Lunatick and Distracted,on the Sixth day of January Instant in the Year aforesaid at the Parish within the Liberty and County aforesaid Pistol made of Iron Steel of the Value of Two Shillings charged with Gunpowder and a Leaden Bullet which she the Said Christiana Randall then and there had and hold in her right hand to and against the head of her the Said Christiana Randall did then and there Shool off and Discharge by means whereof she the said Christiana Randall did then and there give unto herself with the Leaden Bullet aforesaid so Discharged and Shot out of the Pistol aforesaid by the force of the Gunpowder aforesaid in and upon the head of her said Christiana Randall one Mortal Wound of the Breadth of one Inch and of the Death of four Inches of which said Mortal Wound she the said Christiana Randall languished (being speechless) for the Space of about Two Hours and then Died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said, Christiana Randall not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Distracted in manner and by the means aforesaid did Kill herself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place abovementioned

Jno. Feary Coroner .

Tho. Barlow Foreman.
