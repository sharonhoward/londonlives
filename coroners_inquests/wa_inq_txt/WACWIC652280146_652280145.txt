City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex } to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at The Parish of Saint Martin the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Tenth day of March in the twenty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Benjamin Carpenter Esqr then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Gidson Dare , Edward, Thomas Williams , Thomas Greathead William Dunean David Watts , James Neild , James Lock William Watkins , Alexander Palmer , Francis Knight , Francis Relsay Peter Wirgann , John Bland , George Goodwin , John Newcomb John Smith & Joseph Thackary good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Benjamin Carpenter Esqr. came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Benjamin Carpenter labouring under a grevious Disease of Body to Wit a Fever and by reason of the violence of the said Disease being Delirious and out of his Mind on the eighth day of March in the Year aforesaid at the Parish of St. George  Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid to wit into the September River in Hyde Park there did cast and threw himself by means of which said casting and he the said Benjamin Carpenter in the Water of the said River was then and there suffocated and Drowned of which said suffocation and Drowning he the said Benjamin Carpenter then and there instantly died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Benjamin Carpenter by reason of the Disease aforesaid being Delirious and out of his Mind as aforesaid in manner and by the means aforesaid did Drown and kill himself

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Gideon Dure Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day year and Place abovementioned

The Prickard Coroner

Gideon David Foreman
