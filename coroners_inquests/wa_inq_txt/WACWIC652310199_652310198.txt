City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixteenth day of May in the thirty First Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Joseph Dawson then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of Robert More , Mathias Bilger , George Maddock James Pevy , William Arthar , Michael Mc. Meanus , George Cook William Middleton , William Scolthorp , Thomas Pryar , William Woodward John Gittos , John Dixon , Richard Monday , William Lightfoot and William Jones good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Joseph Dawson came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Joseph Dawson (suspected to have been murthered) on the Thirteenth day of May in the Year aforesaid in a certain Court called Cecil Court situate and being in the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life by a Fit of Apoplexy, or other sudden visitation of God in a natural Way.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Robert More  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their present have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Robt More Foreman
