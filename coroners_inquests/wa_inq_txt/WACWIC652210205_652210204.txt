City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty first day of May in the Twenty first Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Corner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of a Male Infant Bastard Child then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of George Snowball , John Wilson , Thomas Hodgson , Christopher Wildman , William Hodgson , Thomas Row , William Winchester , William Stable , John Frushard , George Parkinson , Henry Thompson , Robert Thompson Richard Wynne , Edward Greensill , William Hodgson , Nathaniel Gardner , John Pegg , Charles Townsend , Paul Dupin , Hugh Andrews , Samuel Welsh , Edward Bathorn , Joseph Dancox , and Thomas Smith good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Male Infant Bastard Child came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Male Infant Bastard Child on the Eighteenth day of May in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid did by the Visitation of God then and there Dye a Natural Death and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Forenoon of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Geo: Snowball Foreman
