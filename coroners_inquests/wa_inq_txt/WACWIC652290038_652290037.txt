City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields  Bridewell in the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eighth day of January in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Hannah Robertson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Benjamin , William Miles , Charles Williams , John Gray , James Gardiner , William King , William Hunt John Curtis , Robert Browning , Thomas Hashell , Samuel Studwick and Fisher Andrews good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Hannah Robertson came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Hannah Robertson being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields  Bridewell in the said Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty & County aforesaid on the fifth day of January in the Year aforesaid at Tothill Fields Bridewell aforesaid departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural Way (to wit) of a Dropsy and not otherwise

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Brynnan Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner James Brynnan Foreman
