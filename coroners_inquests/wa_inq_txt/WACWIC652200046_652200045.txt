City and Liberty of Westminster  in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the First day of February in the Twentieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of God Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Graham then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Francis Jones , William Clarke , George Martin , John Lewis , John Fladgate , Simon Life , James Baker , Robert Foulder , William Smith , Peter Henderson , William Rugledge , Thomas Masters and John Perry good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Graham came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said John Graham on the First day of February in the Year aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune fell down into the Area of his dwelling House situate and being in little Maddox Street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, whereby the said John Graham did then and there Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune receive one mortal Bruise in and upon his Neck, of which said mortal Bruise he the said John Graham then and there died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said John Graham in Manner and form aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his death and not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Francis Jones Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovewritten

Tho. Prickard  Coroner Js. Jones Foreman
