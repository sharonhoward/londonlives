City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County Middlesex , the Tenth day of April in the twenty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of James Lane then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Burgan , William Eliner , Savage Ewers John Keekling, Richard Neild , Owen Scott , George Stodart William Baine , William Pixley , Joseph Storer , William Gibson , and Richard Thompson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said James Lane came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said James Lane not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and distracted on the said Tenth day of April in the Year aforesaid into the serpentine River in Hyde park in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid did cast and throw himself by means of which said Casting and throwing he the said James Lane in the Water of the said River was then and these suffocated and Drowned of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said James Lane then and there died

And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said James Lane not being of sound Mind memory and Understanding but Lunatick and Distracted in Manner and by the Means aforesaid did kill himself

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said John Burgan Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place above mentioned

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Jno. Burgan Foreman
