City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Matin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church St. Pater  Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the Thirtieth day of July in the twenty first Year of the Regin of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard . Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Joseph Short then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Strachan , Nathaniel Bone , David Little John Neville , George Parkinson , Isaac Witheat , John Jopham  George Pinner , Joshua Henderson , Thomas Fish Daniel Ross , William Hewson , Robert Taylor , Thomas Pridham and John Smart good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Joseph Short came to h is Death do upon their Oath say, That the said Joseph Short not being of sound Mind, Memory and Understanding, but lunaties and distracted on the twenty ninth day of July in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid a certain Pistol charged with Gunpowder and a leader Bullet which he the said Joseph Short then and there had and held in his Right Hand to and against the Head of him the said Joseph Short did then and there shoot off and discharge by Means whereof he the said Joseph Short did then and there give unto himself with the baden Bullet aforesaid. so discharged and Shot out of the Pistol aforesaid by the fore of the Gunpowder aforesaid, in and through the Head of him the said Joseph Short one mortal wound and Fracture of which said mortal Wound and Fracture, he the said Joseph Short then and there died And so the Jurors aforesaid did upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Joseph short not being of sound mind memory, and Understanding but lunatie and distracted In Manner and by the Means aforesaid did kill himself In Witness whereof as well the Coroner as the said John Strachan Foreman of the said Juror on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their hands & seals the Day Year and Place abovewritten

Tho. Prickard Coroner

John Stranham Foreman
