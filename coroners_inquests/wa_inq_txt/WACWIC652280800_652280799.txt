City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign, Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty ninth day of December in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain,France and Ireland King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman ,Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Sarah Ballentyne then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Walter , Benjamin House , William Shepherd , John Gregory , Jeremiah Hutchins , John Ashbourn , John Lades John Mills , John Macneale , Panton Beten , Samuel Flinders , George Woodman and John Collins good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Sarah Ballentyne came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Sarah Ballentyne on the twenty sixth day of December in the Year aforesaid, was found dead in the Foot way in the Five Fields leading to Chelsea, in the Said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid that no marks of Violence appeared on the Body and that the said Sarah Ballentyne departed this Life in a natural Way, and by no violent means or manner to the Knowledge of the said Jurors.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said John Walter Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

John Walter Foreman
