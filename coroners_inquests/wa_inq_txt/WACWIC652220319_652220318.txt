City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the Sixteenth day of August in the Twenty Second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Harrison a Boy then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Reeve , John Davis , Robert Balfour , Thomas Mason , John Hardy , Valentine Chiddick , Thomas Stanley , Josiah Twitchell , John White , James Allum , Edward Spice , David Davis , and Job Carey good and Lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Thomas Harrison came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Thomas Harrison a Boy of the Age of Ten Years on the Fifteenth day of August in the Year aforesaid at the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty and County aforesaid to wit in a certain pond of Water situate in Tothil Fields going in there to Both himself it so happened that accidentally Casually and by Misfortune he the said Thomas Harrison in the Waters of the said Pond was then and there Suffocated and Drowsed of which said Suffocation and Drowning he that said Thomas Harrison the and other instantly Died. And so the Jurors aforesaid on their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Thomas Harrison in manner and by the Means aforesaid accidentally casually and by Misfortune came to his Death and not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the said Jurors on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner Robt. Reeve Foreman
