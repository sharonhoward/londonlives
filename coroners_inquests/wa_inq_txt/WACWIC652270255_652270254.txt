City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields Bridewell in the Parish of St. Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty first day of May in the twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Nicholson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Matthias Robinson , Thomas Savill , George Staton , John Clark , Charles Williams , William Mence , Robert Greece , William Vincent , William Howard , Thomas Barrett , Robert Archer , John Legdon & John Barrett . good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enqure for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Nicholson came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, that that said John Nicholson being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields Bridewell in the said Parish of St. Margret within the Liberty and County aforesaid on the 20th. day of May in the Year aforesaid departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural way viz ofer Fever and not otherwise

In witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Matthias Robinson  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands & Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Thos Prickard Coroner

Matthias Robinson Foreman
