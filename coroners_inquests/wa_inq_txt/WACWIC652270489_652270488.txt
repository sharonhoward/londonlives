City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twelfth day of November in the twenty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Christian Hambler Guttridge then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Fisher , John Hiett  John Skilton , Robert Sparrow , Francis Nodin , James Smith , Charles Chowles , Charles Lawton , Thomas Butcher , Joseph Fisher , James Storer , and Giles Lawrence good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Christian Hambler Guttridge came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That on the third day of November in the Year aforesaid the said Christian Hambler Guttridge being in a Loft over a Stable situate in Blackburn Mews in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid and then and there standing at the Door of the said Lost and leaning upon a certain Board there fixed It so happened that the said Board get loose and fell down And that the said Christian Hambler Guttridge then and there Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune fell out of and from the said Hayloft upon the Stone Pavement in the said Mews, and thereby did then and there receive a mortal Wound Fracture and Contusion in and upon the left Knee of her the said Christian Hambler Guttridge of which said mortal Wound Fracture and Contusion she the said Christian Hambler Guttridge from the third day of November in the Year aforesaid until the tenth day of the same Month and Year at the said Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid did languish and Live on which said Tenth day of November in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid she the said Christian Hambler Guttridge of the mortal Wound Fracture and Contusion aforesaid did die And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Christian Hambler Guttridge in manner and by the means aforesaid Accidentally Casually & by Misfortune came to her Death and not otherwise

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Fisher Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows on their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Ja Fisher Foreman
