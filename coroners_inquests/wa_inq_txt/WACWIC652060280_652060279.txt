City and Liberty to Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to Wit 

An Inquisition Indented taken for Our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Anne within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster in the County of Middlesex the Eighth day of May in the Sixth year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the Faith and so forth, before Thomas Prickard Gentleman Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, On View of the Body of a New born Female Child then and there lying Dead, upon Oath of Richard Grant , Charles Stace , Archibald Blake , John Sanger , Joseph Ellibank , John Knight , John Milbourne , William Fitz , Henry Hawkins , Thomas Hook , William Hollingworth , James Hopkins and Thomas Cooper , good and lawfull Men of the said Liber duly chosen who being then and there duly Sworn and Charged to inquire for our said Lor the King, when, how and by what Means the said New born Female Child came to her Dea do upon their Oath say, That the said New born Female Child on the Seventh day of May in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid, to Wit, in the Burying Ground belonging to the said Parish, was found Dead; That no Marks of Violence appeared on the Body of the said New born Female Child; And that the said Female Child was Still born. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Richard Grant Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first above written

Thos. Prickard Coroner .

Richd. Grant . foreman
