City of Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex. to Wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter , Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of March in the twenty fifth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the king for the said City and Liberty, on on View of the Body of a Woman unknown then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Hyndes , George Gowing  Joseph Collins , James Urquhart , John King , Benjamin Longmore , James Drummond , John Wenstanley , Thomas Boville , John Tike , Andrew Mattheson , George Fell , Francis Kellor and James Barker good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Woman unknown came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Woman unknown on the said Seventh day of March in the Year aforesaid was found Drowned and Suffocated in the River Thomas near Black Lyon Stairs in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That no Marks of Violence appeared on her Body, but how or by what Means the said Woman whose name is unknown became Drowned an suffocated, no Evidence thereof doth appear to the Jurors.

In Witness Whereof as well the said coroner , as the said Robert Hyndes  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho Prichard Coroner Rob. Hyndes Foreman
