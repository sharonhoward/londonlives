City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields  Bridewell in the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty sixth day of July in the twenty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty,on View of the Body of William Matcalf then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Jordan , Thomas Evans , William Mussery John Dunford , John Neal , John Read , Welter Hodgkinson Thomas Dewick , William Young , Thomas Cox , Thomas Smith and John Weems good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how,and by what Means the said William Metcalf came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said William Metcalf being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields  Bridewell in the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty and County aforesaid on the said twenty sixth day of July in the year aforesaid, at Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a Natural Way to Wit, of a fever and not otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner , as the said William Jordan Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Wm Jordon Foreman
