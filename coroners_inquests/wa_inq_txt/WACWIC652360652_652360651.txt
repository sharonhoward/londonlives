City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex ,} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of William Francis the Sign of the Edinborough Castle in the Strand in the Parish of St. Mary the Strand within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirty first day of October 1796 in the thirty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq. Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Holloway then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Holloway came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said John Holloway on the thirtieth day of October in the year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid being on the Stair-Case at the top of ye House of the said William Francis It so happened that accidentally casually and by Misfortune he the said John Holloway fell over the Bannisters from the said Stair Case to the Ground Floor by Means whereof he the said John Holloway then and there received a Mortal bruise on the back part of the Head of him the said John Holloway : of which said Mortal wound He the said John Holloway on the said John Holloway on the same day, died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their oath aforesaid do say that the said John Holloway in manner and by the Means aforesaid accidentally casually & by Misfortune came to his death & not otherwise.

 IN WITNESS whereof, as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors, have to this Inqusition set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place first above written.

Anthy. Gell Coroner }

Thos Jones Richard Castle Edwd Peaks King Jno Langland
