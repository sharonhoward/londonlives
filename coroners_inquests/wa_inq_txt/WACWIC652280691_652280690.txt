City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit 

An Inquisition Indented taken for Our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Ann within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster in the County of Middlesex the Fifth day of November in the twenty ninth year of the Reign of Our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the Faith and so Forth before Thomas Prickard Gentleman Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty On View of the Body of Jedediah Thomas then lying dead in the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the said Liberty, upon the Oath of Samuel Pidcock , Philip Cristina Smith George Mason , Richard Banks , James Swans , Abraham Hitchins , Thomas Wincott , Nathaniel Elford , Ralph Ellis , John Morton , Thomas Sanders , Samuel Turner , Stephen Le Clere Laurence Whittam , William Grover and Daniel Collinge good and lawfull Men of the said Liberty duly Chosen who being then and there duly Sworn and Charged to inquire for Our said Lord the King, when how, and by what Means the said Jedediah Thomas came to his Death do upon their Oath say That the said Jedediah Thomas on the Thirty first day of October in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of St. Ann within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural Way, and not otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Samuel Pidcock Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hand and Seals the Day year and place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

Samuel Pidcock Foreman
