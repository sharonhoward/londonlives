City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign, Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Margaret (Vizt. at Knightsbridge ) within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of July in the twenty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord a GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner , of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Robert Greenlease then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Makintosh , James Dowdell , William Wood , Jonathan Witchellow , John Hooper , Edward Erwood , John Chappell , William Keeble , Richard Tomkins , Edward Frost , Richard Beedle and Stephen Laxton , good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Robert Greenlease came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Robert Greenlease on the fifth day of July in the Year aforesaid going to bath himself in the Serpentine River in Hyde Park in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, It so happened that Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune he the said Robert Greenlease was in the Water of the said River, then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said Robert Greenlease then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Robert Greenlease in Manner and by the Means aforesaid, accidentally casually and by Misfortune came to his death and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said John Makintosh  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place above written.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Jas Makintosh Foreman
