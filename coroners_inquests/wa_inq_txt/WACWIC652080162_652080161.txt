City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to Wit   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of St Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster  in the County of Middlesex the Third day of May in the Eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the Faith and so forth before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman Coroner of our said Lord the king for the said City and Liberty on View of the Body of Ann Porter then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Jacob Smart  Robert Andrew  Michael Roberts  Andrew Bettey  William Haydon  Thomas Tolley  James Kent  Alexander Farquher , Alexander Gordon  Andrew Pinman  Thomas Mitchel Isaac Releen  James Anderson and John Brown Good and lawful Men of the said Liberty duly chosen who being then and there duly Sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Ann Porter came to her Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Ann Porter not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted on the Second day of May in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid one End of a certain Piece of Smallcord unto an upright piece of Wood supporting a Hanging shelf in a Collar within the Dwelling House of John Straffon situate and being in Lemon- tree Yard in the said Parish Liberty and County and the other End thereof about her own Neck did fix tye and fasten and therewith did then and there hang suffocate and strangle herself of which said Hanging Suffocation and Strangling she the said Ann Porter then and there died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Ann Porter not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick an distracted in Manner and by the Means aforesaid did kill herself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Jacob Smart Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Jacob Smart Foreman
