City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex  to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Fourth day of October in the Sixteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so Forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the king for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of a new born Female Child then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Farlton  Charles Stuart  Job Wakefield  Edward Townshend  James Storling  George Brawn  David Ross  Robert Mason  David Scott  Thomas Parslow  Abraham Hipworth  Thomas Jones  John Lamb  Edward Lee  James Sanders John Allen and William Diffour good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and Charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Female Child came to h er Death, do upon their Oath Say, That the said Female Child on the said Fourth day of October in the Year aforesaid was found dead in a certain Privy or Necessary House belonging to the dwelling House of Edward Fair brother situate in Edward Street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That no Marks of violence appeared on the said Female Child and that the said Female Child was dead born. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Robert Farlton Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho Prickard Coroner

Robt Farlton  Foreman
