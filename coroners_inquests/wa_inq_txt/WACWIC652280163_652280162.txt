City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at The Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventeenth day of March in the twenty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Susannah Dunn then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Hill , John Crook , John Hayn 's, Thomas Watson , Charles Richards , James More , Henry Vincent Thomas Kelly , William Wright , Clayton Wilkinson , Richard Coulthard , Benjamin Reed , Robert Smith , Thomas Norton , Paul Grullier , Richard Cann , Richard Thorn and Andrew Watson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Susannah Dunn came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say , That the said Susannah Dunn not being of sound Mind Memory and understanding but Lunatick and Distracted on the sixteenth day of March in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of Saint James within the Liberty and County aforesaid one End of a certain Peice of small Cord unto an Iron Grate fix'd in the Back Yard belonging to the Dwelling House of William Barns situate and being in the said Parish Liberty and County and the other End thereof about her own Neck did fix tie and fasten and therewith did then and there Hang suffocate and strangle herself of which said hanging suffocation and strangling she the said Susannah Dunn then and there died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Susannah Dunn not being of sound Mind Memory and understanding but Lunatick and Distracted in manner and by the means aforesaid did kill herself

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Hill Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner James Hill Foreman
