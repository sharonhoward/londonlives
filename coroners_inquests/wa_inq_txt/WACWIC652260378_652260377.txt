City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty eighth day of June in the twenty sixth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great- Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Adam Scott then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Reeve , Peter Gough , John Deney James Hamson , William Seal , Charles Williams , William Green , Samuel Townsend , John Ballard , Michael Jefferies , Charles Snooke , Philip Dibley , William Bamfather , William Powell , and Charles Whitrow , good and lawful Men of the Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Adam Scott came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Adam Scott (Suspected to have been Murdered) on the said twenty eighth day of June in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid, departed this Life by the Visitation of you of any Violence of Injury from Richard Edleston or any other Person whatsoever.

In Witness whereof, as well the said Coroner, as the said Robert Reeve  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands, and Seals, the Day, Year and Place above written

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Robt: Reeve Foreman}
