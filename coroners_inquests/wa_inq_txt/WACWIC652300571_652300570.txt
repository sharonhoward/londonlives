City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at The Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventeenth day of September in the Thirtieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of A new born male Child then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Thomas Brown , John Watts , William Todhunter , William Moody , William Weston , Peter, Marshall , William Rodman , Thomas Daffey , John Hendrie , John Fowler , Joseph Mutlow and Daniel Evans good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said New born male Child came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That Martha Miller late of the said Parish of Saint Margaret within the liberty and County aforesaid Singlewoman on the said Seventeenth day of September in the Year aforesaid being then and then big with the said new born male Child afterwards (to wit)on the same Day and year at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid The said Male Child alone and secretly from her Body by the Providence of God did being faith alive, which said Male Child by the Laws and Customs of this Kingdom was a Bastard, And That the said Martha Miller not having the Fear of God before her Eyes but being moved and seduced by the Instigation of the Devil afterwards on the same Day and Year, with Force and Arms at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid in and upon the said New born Male Child so alive and in the Peace of God and of our said lord the King then and there being Feloniously wilfully and of her Malice forethought did make an Assault, And That the said Martha Miller , him the said new born male Child did then and there take into both her Hands, and him the said new born male Child into a certain Privy or Water Closet there situate, then and then feloniously wilfully and of her malice forethought did violently cast and throw down by means whereof he the said New born Male Child in the Soil or Filth then and there continued in the said Privey or Water Closet was then and there suffocated and smothered of which said Suffocate and smothering he the said New born Male Child then and then instantly died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, That the said Martha Willer him the said new born male Bastard Child in manner and by the means aforesaid feloniously Wilfully and her malice forethought did kill and murder, against the Peace of our said Lord the King his and Dignity

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Thomas Brown Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of this Fellow in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

W Brown Foreman
