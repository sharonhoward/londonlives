City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex ,} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of the late John James Rosslett the Sign of the Golden Lion  Dean Street in the parish of St Anne Soho within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the second day of January 1799 in the thirty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of Body of the said John James Rosslett then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how and by what Means the said John James Rosslett came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say that the said John James Rosslett not being of sound Mind Memory & Understanding but lunatic and distracted on the said second day of James att he Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid one end of a certain Jucee of small Cord unto an Iron Nail fixed in the bellar belonging to the Dwelling House of him the said John James Rosslett and the other End thereof about his own Neck did fix lye & fasten & therewith did then and that hang and saffocate himself Of which said Hansing Saffocation he the said John James Rosslett then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said John James Rosslett not being of sound Mind Memory and Under standing but lunatic and distracted in manner and by the Means aforesaid did kill himself.

 IN WITNESS whereof, as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors, have to this Inqusition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year, and Place first above written.

Anthy Gell Coroner } Wm Polls Phil Adams Samuel Alton atta Stanly Wa rick

Alhard Archd. Brunton Stephn. Callahan Thomas Lucas James Swain Jas Smart Charles Cunningham
