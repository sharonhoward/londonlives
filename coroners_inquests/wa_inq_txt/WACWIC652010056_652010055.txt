Westminster City and Liberty in the County of Middlesex } to wit An Inquisitioniberty of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter at Westminster the Eleventh day of September in the frist Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the faith and so forth, and in the Year of our Lord One Thousand seven hundred and sixty one . Before John Feary Coroner for our Sovereign Lord the King for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid Upon a View of the Body of Mary Rayner then and there lying Dead by the Oaths of Thomas Palmer , Joseph Knight , John Wilson , John Felix , John Lacy Richard Kelsby  Henry Capell , John Boultabs , William Marshall , William Rose , Edward Whitaker , Charles Churchman , John Smith , John Dunn , Andrew Webson , John Haynes and John Lancaster Good and Lawfull Men of the said City and Liberty who being Sworn and Charged to Enquire for our Sovereign Lord the King how when where and in what manner the said Mary Rayner came to her Death say upon their Oaths. That the said Mary Rayner not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and Distracted on the Ninth day of September Instant in the Year aforesaid at the Parish Liberty and County aforesaid a great quantity of Liquid Landanum being a deadly Poison did then and there take drink and swallow down by means whereof she the said Mary Rayner became then and there sick and distempered in her Body and of the Poison aforesaid and of the Sickness and Distemper thereby occasioned from the hour of Ten in the Morning of the same day untill about half an hour after Twelve in the Morning of Thursday the tenth of this Instant September did languish and languishing did Live on which Said Tenth day of September in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid she the said Mary Rayner of the Poison aforesaid and of the Sickness and Distemper occasioned thereby did Dye. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Mary Rayner not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and Distracted in manner and by the mean Before said Poison and Kill herself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the day Year and at the Place abovementioned.

Jno. Feary Coroner .

Thos Palmer Foreman
