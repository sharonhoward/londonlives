City of Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex . } to wit.

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Fourth day of November in the Twenty Seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William Mark Wilkinson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Joseph Malpas , John Salt , John Walter , Richard Kirkham William Dean , William Cain , Richard Popwell , Benjamin Curtis , Thomas Reynaldson , Thomas Rising , James Ansell , and James Campbele good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said William Mark Willkison came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said William Mark Willkison a Child of the Age of Four Months on the Third day of November in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid did then and there by the Visitation of God Dye or Natural Death. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Jos: Malpas Foreman
