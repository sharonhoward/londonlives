City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex .} to wit  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the House of William Francis Sign of the Edinburgh Castle in the Strand , Parish of St. Mary le Strand within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty first day of June 1794 in the thirty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Lyddy Ann Wortley then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are hereunder written and Seals affixed good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Lyddy Ann Wortley came to her Death, do upon their Oath say Lyddy Anne Wortley, not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but lunatic and distracted on the twenty fourth day of June in the Year aforesaid at the parish and in the Liberty aforesaid one End of a certain Price of small Cord unto a Hook fastgr in the Ceiling, in the Lodging Room or Apartment of her the said Lyddy Anne Wortley, in the Dwilling House of Farrel in Pettyford Yard Little Druy, Lane, and the other End thereof about her own Neck, did fix, tye, and faster, and there with did then and there hang, suffocate, and strangle herself: Of which said Hanging, Suffocation and Strangling she the said Lyddy, Ann Wortley then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Lyddy Ann Wortley not being of sound Mind, Memory and Understanding, but lunatic and distracted in manner & by the means aforesaid, did kill herself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their hands & Seals the day Year and place first above written

Anthy. Gell Coroner } Thos Jones  Foreman James Lorremore Owen Stratton Geo Todd Willm Warburton Richard Boulton

John Goodings Danl Ward C Melanschy Saml Little Josh Hurst H Wright
