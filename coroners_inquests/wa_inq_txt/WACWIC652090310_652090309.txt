City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of St James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty first day of July 1769 in the Ninth year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, king, Defender of the Faith, and so Forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the king for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Joshua Prestage then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Price Jasper Value , Thomas Hill , Joseph Newton  Thomas Tulford  Charles Rider , Anthony Frith  William Thompson  Thomas Reddish  Thomas Little  Richard Barratt  William White John Heath  James Rutt  Samuel Montford , Samuel Wood , and Tristin Fletcher good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and Charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Joshua Prestage came to his Death, do upon their Oath Say,

That the said Joshua Prestage not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted on the Twentieth day of July in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid in a certain Room called the Auction Room part of the dwelling House of James Christie Auckinson there situate with a certain Knife made of Iron and Steel which he the said Joshua Prestage then and there had and held in his Right hand, the Throat or Gullet of him the said Joshua Prestage did then and there Strike Stab and penatrate thereby, then and there giving unto himself the said Joshua Prestage with the Knife aforesaid in and upon the Throat or Gullet of him the did Joshua Prestage one Mortal Wound of the Length of Three Inches and of the Depth of One Inch of which said Mortal wound he the said Joshua Prestage did Languish and Languishing did Live for the space of half an Hour or thereabouts, and then in the Dwelling House of the said J nes Christie in the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid Died And to the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do Say that the said Joshua Prestage not being of Sound Mind Memory and understanding but Lunatic and distracted in manner and by the Means aforesaid did Kill himself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the day and Year first above written

Tho Prickard Coroner

John Price Foreman
