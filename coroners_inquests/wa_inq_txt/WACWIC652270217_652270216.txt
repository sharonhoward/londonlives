City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of he Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Nineteenth day of April in the twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third,by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner or our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty on View of the Body of Hugh Pugh then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Tomlinson , Hambleton Thomas Tayler Philip Hatsell , Robert Frampton , John Penton Goter , James Marke , Thomas Starkey , Thomas Ralph , Corneluis Davidson Jacob Betts , Joshua Davis , Richard Hambley , William Dodds , & Robert Young . good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and then duly Sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when , how, and by what Means the said Hugh Pugh came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Hugh Pugh on the Seventeenth day of April in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life by excessive Drinking & not from any Hurt Injury or Violence done or Committed by John Edwards or any other Person whatsoever to the Knowledge of the said Jurors

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Tomlinson  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inqusition set their Hands & Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho Prickard  Coroner

James Tomlinson  Foreman
