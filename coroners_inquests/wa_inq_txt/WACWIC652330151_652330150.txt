City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Intended, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Dwelling House of William Proctor in Henrietta Street , at the Parish of St. Pauls Covent Garden Sign of the within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eleventh day of March 1793  in the thirty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Sarah Peak then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Walter Watkins , James Mason , Peter Vincent  Evan Evans John Manson , John Williams , Edward Smith , Matthew Ward , William Humphreys , John Nisbet , John Merrick , Benjamin Abbott , Edward Winstanley and Joseph FreshWater good and lawful Men of the said Liberty duly chosen, who being then and there duty sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Sarah Peak came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Sarah Peak on the tenth day of March in the year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid To Wit in Queen's Court King Street deponent this Life through the Inclemency of the Weather and by no violent Ways or Means whatsoever to the Knowledge of the said Jurors.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors leave to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the Day year and Place first above Written

Anthy Gell Coroner }

Foreman James Mason Peter Vincent
