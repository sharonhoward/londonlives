City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middelsex.} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Clement Danes within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Tenth day of April in the twenty sixth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grave of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Seldon then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Fossett , James Smart . Benjamin Johnson . Joseph Cartridge , Prichard Day , Matthew Sowby , Edward Baker , Edmond Brickledon , Samuel Bentton , Samuel Atkinson . John Tate , James Stevens , Robert Fearnley , William Dawes . and Thomas Comb good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Seldon came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said John Seldon on the Eighth day of April in Year aforesaid, at the said Parish of Saint Clement Danes within the Liberty and County aforesaid, Departed this Life by Excessive drinking and not Otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said William Fossett  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place above written

Tho Prickard Coroner

Wm. Fassett Foreman }
