City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Midddlesex, the Twenty ninth day if January in the Twenty Seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King Defender of the Faith, and so Forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of a New Born Male Infant Child then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Cole , John Cocks , John Dalby , George Watson , Philip Mettivier , James Patterson , James Gibbs , Robert Gilron, Evan Evans , William Prior  George Darling , Thomas Hookham , Samuel Furley , William Moore and Abraham Streggs good and Lawful Men of the Said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duty sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said New Born Male Infant Child came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That on the Twenty seventh day of January in the year aforesaid the said New Born Male Infant Child was found Dead in the Privey or Neccessary in the Dwelling House of Richard Robinson  Esquire Situate in New Bond Street in the Parish aforesaid without the Liberty and County aforesaid, that no Marks of Violence appeared on the Body of the said New Born Male Infant Child, and that the said Male Child was Still Born In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day year and at the Place first above mentioned.

John Cocks John Dalley  G Watson Philip Mativin James Patterson James Gibbs Robt Gibon

W. Pryer Geo. Darling Samuel Farley William Moore Abr. Shegg

Tho Prickard Coroner John Cole Foreman
