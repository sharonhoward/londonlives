City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of May in the Nineteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Empson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of George Kershaw , John Roberts , Charles Fant , William Saurey , Richard Cunliff , Hammond Myers , John Walker , Thomas Price , John Sutton , Thomas Hiller , John Cranck , William Taylor , Thomas Hopkins , and John Taylor good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Thomas Empson came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Thomas Empson not being of Dund Mind Memory and Understanding, but lunatick and distracted, on the Seventh day of May in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, One End of a certain Leather Strap unto a Wooden Rail fastened into the Wainscot in the Shop of Francis Addy situate and being in the said Parish Liberty and County, and the other End thereof about his own Neck did fix tye and fasten, and therewith did then and there hang suffocate and strangle himself, of which said Hanging Suffocation and Strangling he the said Thomas Empson then and there died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Thomas Empson , not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted, in Mayner and by the Means aforesaid did kill himself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said George Kershaw Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho Prickard Coroner

Geo: Kershaw Foreman
