An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eleventh day of May in the twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain,France and Ireland King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Ann Galway then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of David Stoddart , Thomas Fenns , Robert Walker John Holland , Thomas Gray , John Adams , George Ludell , Joseph Exter , William Sheat , William Pixley , Charles Jenkins & Henry Slack good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Ann Gatway came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Ann Galway not being of sound Mind, Memory and understanding but Lunatick & distracted on the thirtieth day of April in the year aforesaid into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park at the said Parish of St. George  Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid did cast and thow herself by means of which said casting & throwing she the said Ann Galway in the Water of the said river was then & there suffocated and Drowned of which said suffocation and Drowning she the said Ann Galway then and there instantly died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid so say that the said Ann Galway in manner & by the Means aforesd. not being of sound Mind Memory & Understanding but Lunatick and distracted did Drown and till herself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said David Stoddart  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself & the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands & Seals the Day Year & Place abovementioned

Thos Prickard Coroner

David Stoddart Foreman
