City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the twenty eighth day of December in the twenty fifth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Elizabeth Prothero then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of George Kershaw , John James , Edward Lewis Joseph Hill , John Moody , Robert Hewitt , William Catliff , William Newton , William Watson , David Smeller , Charles Young , James Thomas and Giles Weller good and Lawfull Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duty Sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Elizabeth Prothers came to her Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Elizabeth Prothers not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted on the said Twenty eighth day of December in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid in the Dwelling House of John Woons there Situate with a certain Razor made of Iron Street which she the said Elizabeth Prothero then and then had and held in her Right Hand, the Throat or Gullet of her the said Elizabeth Prothers did then and there strike stab and Penetrate thereby then and there giving unto herself with the Razor aforesaid in and upon the Throat or Gullet of her the said Elizabeth Prothers one mortal wound of the length of three Inches and of the Depth of one Inch, of which said mortal wound she the said Elizabeth Prothero then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their oath aforesaid do say, that the said Elizabeth Prothers, not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted, in Manner and form aforesaid did kill herself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said George Kershaw  Foreman of the said, Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Thos. Prickard  Coroner .

Geo: Kershaw Foreman
