City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean the Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Fourth day of February in the twenty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Poole then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Astley , Thomas Oliver , John Brutton Abraham Skeggs , Jeremiah Bullock , Edward Ball Matthew Clark , James Kennedy , Robert Gihon , William Pearson Caleb Atkinson , Myrry Wilson , Thomas Masters , and Charles Logan good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Poole came to h is, Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said John Poole on the Thirty first day of January in the Year aforesaid (at Night) was found dead in a certain Publick Street called little Maddox Street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, And that the said John Poole departed this Life in a Fit or other sudden Visitation of God, in a natural Way, In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said John Astley  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place above written.

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

John Astley Foreman
