City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields  Bridewell in the Parish of St Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of December in the twenty fifth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Lucas a Prisoner then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of David Davies  James Hemett , John Skilling Robert Love , Michael Francis , George Laywood , Thomas Arnold , Thomas Wetherall , Samuel Tredwick , Samuel Grimsall , John Haker , Richard Carter , William Ame and William White good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Lucas came to his Death, do upon their Oath say , That the said John Lucas being a Prisoner in Tothill Fields Bridewell aforesaid in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, on the Seventh day of December in the Year aforesaid at Tothill Fields Bridewell aforesaid departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a Natural Way, to wit, of a Fever and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said David Davies  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard Coroner . David Davis Foreman
