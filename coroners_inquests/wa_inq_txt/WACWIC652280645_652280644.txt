City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex . } to wit. 

An Inquisition Indednted. taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at The Parish of Saint John the Evangelist within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty fifth day of October in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Jane Wade then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Henry Webb , Aquila Cole , Peter Crocker , William Menzies , Henry Tainsh , William Abell , William Bonnycastle James Clubb , Theobald Archer , Joseph Salter , Samuel Townsend John Strudwell and William Carvill good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Jane Wade came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Jane Wade (suspected to have been murthered) On the twentieth day of October in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of St. John the Evangelist within the Liberty and County aforesaid, suddenly departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural Ways and not by any Violence or Injury from John Wade Husband to the said Jane Wade, or any other person whatsoever to the Knowledge of the said Jurors.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner , as the said Henry Webb Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day year and Place abovementioned.

Tho Prickard Coroner

Henry Webb Foreman
