City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eighteenth day of January in the Twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so fourth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Henry Lea then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Thomas Alstone , George Coleman , Joseph Carron , Adam Armstrong Matthew Hill , David Allen , Mathews Robinson, William Atwood , Donald Mac Donald  Peter Powell , Charles Chance , William Robert , and John Griffiths good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Henry Lea came to h is Death, upon their Oath say That the said Henry Lea not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted, on the Seventeenth day of January in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish aforesaid in the liberty and County aforesaid of Small Cord unto a Brass Hook faster into the Wainsert in a Room in the Dwelling House of the said Henry Lea Situate on Brewers Green in the said Parish Liberty and County and the other and thereof about his own Neck did Fix tye and fasten, and therewith did then and there Hang Suffocate and Strangle himself of which said Hanging Suffocation and Strangling he the said Henry Lea then and there Died. And to the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say. That the said Henry Lea not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatic and Distracted in manner and by the Means aforesaid did Kill himself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid, or behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and at the Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Thos. Foreman
