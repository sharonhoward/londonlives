City & Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlsex } to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for out Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Clement Danes within the Liberty of the Dean Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eleventh day of April in the Ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Jeremiah Downes then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Edward Bertles , James Holl , Thomas Bayly , William Crockford , James Bulgins , Alexander Anderson , Philip Nobs , John Williams  James King , John Gray , John Green , William Richardson , James Duke , Thomas Hudson , Henry Phillips , Humphrey Lloyd and Francis Racker good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when how, and by what Mean the said Jeremiah Downs came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Jeremiah Downs (suspected to have been Murdered) on the Tenth day of April in the Year aforesaid, died suddenly in a place called Star Court in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That no Marks of Violence appeared on his Body, and that the said Jeremiah Downs died, by the Visitation of God in a natural Way, and not by any Hurt or Injury form any Person whatsoever. In Witness where of as- well the said Coroner, as the said Edward Bertles Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Edward Bertles Foreman
