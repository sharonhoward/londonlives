City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eleventh day of February in the twenty second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William Ogle then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Matthew Pearson , William Guard , John Withers , Charles Ellot  Jacob Beere Thomas Grace  John Gould  Jeremiah Harvey , John Paul , John Margram , James Baden , John Martin Dyshkin , James Leslie , John Sorrell , Thomas Weston , George Forley , Moses Lany , Thomas Harrison , and John Smellie good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said William Ogle came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said William Ogle and another Man to the Jurors aforesaid unknown on the Fourth day of February in the Year aforesaid with Force and Arms at the Parish of in the County aforesaid in and upon one Robert Mitford in the Peace of God and of our said Lord the King and in the King's Highway then and there being feloniously did make an Assault, and him the said Robert Mitford in Bodily fear and Danger of his Life in the Kings Highway aforesaid then and there feloniously did put, and the Goods Chattels and Monies of the said Robert Mitford from the Person and against the Will of the said Robert Mitford in the King Highway aforesaid then and there feloniously did steal take and carry away against the Peace of our said Lord the King his brown and Dignity Whereupon the said Robert Mitford then and there having a certain Pistoll of the Value of five Shillings in his Right Hand loaded with Gun powder and a leaden Bullet he the said Robert Mitford the same Pistol to and against the said William Ogle in Defence of himself and his Property did then and there lawfully and Justifiably shoot off and discharge and him the said William Ogle with the leaden Bullet aforesaid so discharged from and out of the said Pistol by force of the Gunpowder aforesaid in and upon his right Breast did then and three Strike Wound and Penetrate Giving to the said William Ogle then and there in and upon his said Right Breast one mortal Wound of the breadth of one Inch and of the depth of six Inches of which said mortal Wound he the said William Ogle at the Parish last mentioned and also at the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty and County aforesaid from the said Fourth day of February in the Year aforesaid until the Eighth day of the same Month in the same Year did languish and languishing did live on which said Eighth day of February in the Year aforesaid the said William Ogle at the said Parish of Saint Paul Covent Garden within the Liberty and County aforesaid of the said Mortal Wound did die, And that the said Robert Mitford him the said William Ogle in manner and form aforesaid, in Defence of himself and his Property did lawfully and Justifiably kill and slay.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Matthew Pearson Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day year and Place above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Mattw. Pearson Foreman
