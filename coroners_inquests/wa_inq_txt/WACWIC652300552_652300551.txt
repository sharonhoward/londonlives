City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at The Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirteenth day of September in the Thirtieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Griffiths then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Joseph Higgs , James Brodie , Peter Fozard , William Gannell William Conyer , Robert Seabrook , Edward Physick , William Blower John Kemp , Jeremiah Carraway , William Walker , James Mitchell and John Piddington good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Thomas Griffiths came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That on the Eleventh day of September in the Year aforesaid the said Thomas Grifiths was riding a certain Bay Gelding in Hyde Park in the said Parish of St. George  Hanover Square within the Liberty and County aforesaid, and going to Water the said Horse in th e Serpentine River there, It so happened that the said Horse slipt into a Hole in the said River, and that the said Thomas Griffiths Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune was then and there suffocated and Drowned of which said suffocation and Drowning he the said Thomas Griffiths then and there died, And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Thomas Griffiths Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his Death and not otherwise

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Joseph Higgs Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Joseph Higg Foreman
