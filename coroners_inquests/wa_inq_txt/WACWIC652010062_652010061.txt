Westminster City and Liberty in the County of Middlesex } to wit An Inquisition Indented and taken at the Parish of St. Martin in the feilds in the Liberty of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter at Westminster the Fourteenth day of October in the Frist Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the faith and so forth, and in the Year of our Lord One Thousand seven hundred and sixty one . Before John Feary Corner for our Sovereign Lord the King for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid. Upon a View of the Body of Elijah Hannah then and there lying Dead, by the Oaths of Edward Davis , John Haynes , James Jones , John Flinn , John Hawksworth , John Robinson , John Hard , Joseph Smith , William Wolfe , Thomas Hayes , John Lightning , John Platt , John Davis Robert Berry , John Groleman , William Abbott , and William Marcellus Good and Lawfull Men of the said City and Liberty who being Sworn and Charged to Enquire for our Sovereign Lord the King how when where and in what manner the said Elijah Hannah came to his Death say upon their Oaths That Stephen Dean late of the Parish aforesaid in the Liberty and County aforesaid, Gentlemen on the Tenth day of October Instant in the Year aforesaid with Force and Arms at the Parish Liberty and County aforesaid in and upon the said Elijah Hannah in the Peace of God and of our said Lord the King then and there being Feloniously did make an Assault, And that the said Stephen Dean with a certain drawn Sword made of Iron and Steel of the Value of Five Shillings which he the said Stephen Dean then and there had and held in his Right hand him the said Elijah Hannah in and upon the breast of him the said Elijah Hannah did and there Feloniously strike and Stab, And that the said Stephen Dean with the Sword aforesaid did then and there him the said Elijah Hannah in and upon the said Breast of him the said Elijah Hannah one Mortal Wound of the Breadth of One Inch and of the Depth of Four inches of which said Mortal Wound he the said Elijah Hannah from the said Tenth day of October until the Twelfth day of the Same Month in the year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid in the Liberty and County aforesaid did Languish and Languishing did Live on which said Twelfth day of October in the Year aforesaid at the Parish Liberty and County aforesaid did Dye, And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Stephen Dean him the said Elijah Hannah in manner and by the means aforesaid Feloniously did Kill and Stay against the Peace of our said Lord the King his Crown and Dignity. And that the said Stephen Dean at the time of the doing and Committing of the Felony and Manslaughter aforesaid or at any time since had no Goods or Chattels lands or Tenements within the said Liberty or elsewhere to the Knowledge or Notice of the said Jurors In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition Set their hands and Seals the day Year and at the Place abovementioned.

Jno. Feary Coroner .

Edwd. Davis Foreman
