City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixth day of January in the twenty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Daniel whose Surname is unknown then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Price , William Chandless , Thomas Reeve David Wernham , John Robinson , John Woodman , Richard Ladd , Nathan Carrington , John Cooke John Miller , Abraham Musgrave and Thomas Storer , good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Daniel came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Daniel (whose Surname is unknown) a Laborer on the said Sixth day of January in the Year aforesaid being at Work on the outside of an Empty House in John Street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, and being then and there upon the Parapet in the Front of the said House It so happened that the said Daniel Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune do then and there fall from thence into the said Street, and thereby did then and there receive a Mortal Fracture in and upon the back part of his Head, of which said Mortal Fracture he the said Daniel then and there instantly died, and so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Daniel in Manner and form aforesaid, Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his Death, and not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Price  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place first abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

James Price Foreman
