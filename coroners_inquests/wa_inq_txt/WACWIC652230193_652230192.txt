City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex . } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the First day of Mary in the twenty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Andrew Ferguson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Daniel James , Andrew Smith , Martin Smith Richard Hare , John Jones , James Blanch , Charles Ross , Henry Mc. Donald, William Fisher  Philip Barton , Charles Hinrich , George Parkerson , Joseph Stevens , Thomas Gosheron and Thomas Hodson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Andrew Ferguson came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Andrew Ferguson on the Thirtieth day of April in the year aforesaid was found drowned in the River Thames near Ivy Bridge, in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That the said Andrew Ferguson had no Marks of violence appearing on his Body, but how or by what Means he became Drowned and Suffocated, no Evidence there of doth appear to the Jurors. In Witness where of as well the said Coroner as the said Daniel James  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and place above written.

Tho. Prickard Coroner Daniel James Foreman
