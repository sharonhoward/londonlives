City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit. 

In Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Tothill Fields  Bridewell in the Parish of St. Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty seventh day of October in the Twenty first Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Elizabeth Pye a Prisoner then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Durrand , Matthew Todd , Thomas Tindall , Robert Squire , William Ashdon , Anthony Fabery , Joseph Niblow , Joseph Mabley , James Chalk , Richard Ramsey , Thomas Bowen and John Hudson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Elizabeth Pye came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Elizabeth Pye a Prisoner in Tothill Fields  Bridewell aforesaid, in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, on the Twenty- sixth day of October in the Year aforesaid at Tothill Fields Bridewell aforesaid departed this Life by the Nisitation of God in a Natural Way, to Wit, of a Fever, and not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said John Durrand Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovewritten

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

John Durrand Foreman
