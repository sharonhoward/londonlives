City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the fourth day of December in the twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain,France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Anthony Tourdan , John Lewis , John Bradford Henry Curwen , Walter Evans , John Thomson , John Gregory William Gimson , Miles Jones , Edward Earle , Thomas Leach and John Cory Good and lawful Men of the said Liberty. duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, That on the said Fourth day of December in the Yeat aforesaid, the said Man unknown was found Drowned and Suffocated in the River Thames at York Buildings in the Parish aforesaid, within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That the said Man unknown had no Mark of Violence appearing on his Body, but how and by what Means he became Drowned and Suffocated no Evidence thereof doth of appear to the Jurors.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Anthony Tourdan Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their Presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place above mentioned

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Anthony Tourdan  Foreman
