City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eighth day of November in the Thirty Second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Martha Sherman then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of William Payne , John Burton , John Walker , William Gee Thomas Clout , John Mc. Intoch , William Allden , Thomas Betsworth William Slater , John Williamson , Robert Atkinson , John Greenlow William Thomas and Daniel Bruckley good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Martha Sherman came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say, That on the Seventh day of November in the Year aforesaid the said Martha Sherman was found dead in a certain Court called Old round Court in the said Parish of St. Martin in the Fields within the Liberty and County aforesaid and that the said Martha Sherman reported this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural Way and not by any Violence or Injury whatsoever

In Witness whereof as will the said Coroner as the said William Payne  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner .

Wm Payne  Foreman
