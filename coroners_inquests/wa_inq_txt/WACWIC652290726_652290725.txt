City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at The Parish Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eighth day of December in the Thirtieth, Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Richard Cooper then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Joseph Mathers , Ebenzer Buchanan , William Ucghorn Joseph Dickson , Henry Deverell , Henry Bankin , Richard Hillyer Charles Hawkes , William Huggins , William Attwood , James Conyers John Maris and John Watson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Richard Cooper came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Richard Cooper a Carpenter on the Fifteenth day of December in the Year aforesaid, being at Work at the Admirally office, situate in the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields , within the County aforesaid, and coming up the stairs leading out of the Garden into the Hall, It so happened that the said Richard Cooper then and there, Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune fell from the said stairs into the Area on the bach part of the said building, by Means whereof the said Richard Cooper received a Mortal Fracture on the left side of his stead and a concession in the Brain of him the said Richard Cooper ,of which said Mortal Fracture and concession he the said Richard Cooper from the said Fifth day of December in the Year aforesaid until the seventh day of the same Month and Year at the said Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty and County aforesaid did languish and live on which said Seventh day of December in the Year aforesaid at the Parish last mentioned he she said Richard Cooper of the Mortal Fracture and Concession aforesaid did Die And so she Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Richard Cooper in Manner and by the Means aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his Death and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Joseph Mathers Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the art of his fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard  Coroner Josh Mathers Foreman
