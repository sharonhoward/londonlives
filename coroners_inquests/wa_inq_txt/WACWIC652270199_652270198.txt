City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint Mary le Strand within the liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter , Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twelfth day of April in the twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so froth, before, Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Bintt then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Jones  Thomas Banks  Thomas Westby Thomas Jones  Hardy Vaux  James Tomkins  John Gast John Wisker  Joseph Bengough  John Williams  John Molloy John Fawcett  William Bushby  John Goodings  William Fairman  William Rose  Charles Lettellier  William Hornbuchle James Dakers & William Begbie good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Bintt came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said John Bintt not being of sound Mind Memory & Understanding but Lunatick and distracted on the Eleventh day of April in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid one End of a certain Price of small Cord unto the top of one of the Bed posts in the Lodging Room or Apartment of him the said John Bintt in the Dwelling House of James Ogilvie situate in Swan Yard in the said Parish Liberty and County and the other End thereof about his own neck did fix tye and fasten and there with did then and there hang suffocate and strangle himself of which said Hanging Suffocation and Strangling he the said John Bintt then and there died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said John Bintt not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and Distracted in Manner and by the Means aforesaid did hill himself In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Robert Jones Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their Presence have to this Inqusitions set their Hands and Seals the Day Year & Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Robt. Jones  Foreman
