City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Ninth day of April in the twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender, of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Simon Summers then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Newland  Jacob Tayler  John Bates Richard Randall  Thomas Stikeman  John Ungerland Thomas Pendril  William Rownas  John Jones Philip Van Swinden  John Clark  Richard Wardell  Thomas Lee good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Simon Summers came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Simon Summers on the Eighth day of April in the Year aforesaid was going along Charlotte Street Pimlico at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid and being Intoxicated with Liquor It so happened that the said Simon Summers then & there accidentally casually & by Misfortune fell into an then Ditch or Sworn at the End of the said street and in the Water in the said S ers Suffocated & Drowned of which said Suffocation & Drowning he the said Simon Summers then & there died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Simon Summers in Manner and by the Means aforesaid Accidentally casually and by Misfortune came to his Death & not otherwise In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Newland  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands & Seals the Day Year & Place above mentioned

The Prickard Coroner

James Newland  Foreman
