City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint John the Evangelist within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of August in the Sixteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William Edward Da Costa then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Watson John Granger  William Huntly , Joseph Gartick , Thomas Staples , William Ower , Humphrey Hughes , Thomas Green , George Dike , James Lloyd , John Wright , William Green , Abraham Appleton , Gilbert Lawson , and John White good and Lawfull Men of the said Liberty duly chosen, who being then and there duty Sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said William Edward Da Costa came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said William Edward Da Costa on the Sixth day of August in the Year aforesaid at the Parish, City and Liberty and County aforesaid to wit at the Neal Houses on Milbank going in to the River Thomas there to Bathe himself, it so happened that accidentally Casually and by Misfortune he the said William Edward Da Costa was in the Waters of the said River, then and there suffocated and Drowned at which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said William Edward Da Costa then and there instantly Died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said William Edward Da Costa in manner and by the means aforesaid accidentally casually and by Misfortune came to his Death and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid on behalf of himself and the rest of the said Jurors have to this Inquisition set their Hands, and Seals the Day Year and at the Place first abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Robt Watson Foreman
