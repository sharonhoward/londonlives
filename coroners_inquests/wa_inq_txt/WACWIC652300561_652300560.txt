City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at The Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Fourteenth day of September in the Thirtieth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Susannah Heighington then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Lloyd , John Lorzier , William Nisbett , James Adnum James Tickridge , John Gwytt , Charles Newman , Richard Rodwell Richard Gardener , William French , George Davison , John Mayhew , Christopher Bullin , and William Dabney good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Susannah Heighington came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Susannah Heighington not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and distracted, on the Twelfth day of September in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of St. James within the Liberty and County aforesaid one End of a certain Peie of Cord unto an Iron Hook fastened into the Waincot in the Lodging Room or Apartment of her the said Susannah Heighington in the Dwelling House of Mr Davey situate and being in Carnaby Street in the said Parish Liberty and county and the other End thereof about her own neck did fit tie and fasten and therewith did then and there hang suffocate and strangle herself, of which said hanging suffocation and strangling she the said Susannah Heighington then and there died, And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Susannah Heighington in manner and by the means aforesaid not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding Vat Lunatick and distracted did kill herself

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said William Lloyd  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho Prickard Coroner

Wm Lloyd Foreman
