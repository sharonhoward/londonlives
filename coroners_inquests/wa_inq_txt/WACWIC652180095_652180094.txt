City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  

 An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at The Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixth day of April in the Eighteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Sarah Winter then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Townsend , Thomas Legg , John Lewis  Samuel Battman , Thomas Haley , John Midland  Charles Bishop  John Wilson , Barnard Marr , John Joison , Daniel Bergman , Joseph Old and Peter Henderson good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what means the said Sarah Winter came to her Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Sarah Winter , Not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but lunatick and distracted, on the fourteenth day of March in the year aforesaid into a certain Baron in Hyde Park in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, there did cast and throw herself, by means of which said Casting and Throwing she the said Sarah Winter in the Water in the said Bason was then and there suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Downing she the said Sarah Winter then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid dy say that the said Sarah Winter not being of sound Mind Memory and understanding but lunatick and distracted, in Manner and by the Means aforesaid did kill and drown herself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said William Townsend Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned.

Tho. Prickard  Coroner

Wm. Townsend Foreman
