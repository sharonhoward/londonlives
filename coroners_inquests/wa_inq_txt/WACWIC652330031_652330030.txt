City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex .} to wit  

 An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Dwelling House of William Taylor in Ryley Street in Ye Parish of St. Martin in the Fields Sign of the King's Head within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Ninth day of January 1793 in the Thirty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq . Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William Dorand then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Anderson , John Rutherford , Thomas Greenaway Samuel Vizard  John Strong , Isaac Clemett , Peter Pike  William Hargrave  Joseph Pedder , John Hall  Thomas Jones , Thomas William Richard Hopkins and Samuel Knox good and lawful Men of the Said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said William Durand came to his Death, do upon their Oath Say that the said William Durand , on the Eighth day of January in the Year aforesaid, at the Parish and in the Liberty aforesaid departed this Life by the visitation of God in a Natural way, To wit of a Fit, and not otherwise

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors came to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the Day Year & Place above written.

Anthy Gell  Coroner Andrews S. Vn Peter Pike

Jso. Clemmett J William Richd. Hopkins Thos Jones Jan. Thawe William Elangeare

John Hart Joseph Peday John Rutherford
