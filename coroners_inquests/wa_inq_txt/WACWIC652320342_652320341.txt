City of Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint James Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventh day of June 1792 in the thirty second Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and ireland, King Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Hanna Hearshaw then and there lying dead , upon the Oath of James Small , John Rickerly , Willaim harding  William Thomas  Thomas Sutherland , Paul Grellier  Robert Hamilton , William Roberts , John Barley , William Brown , Thomas Haworth , John Newman , John Thompson , Charles Haslegrave , and Thomas Davis good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Hanna Kearshaw came to her Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Hanna Kearshaw, not being of sound Mind, Memory, and understanding, but lunatic and distracted, on the Sixth day of june, in the Year aforesaid at the parish and in the Liberty aforesaid, To Wit, at the dwelling House of Joseph Kearshaw (her Husband ) there situate with a certain Razer made of iron and Steel, which she the said Hanna Kearshaw then and there had and held, the Throat or Gullet of her the said Hanna Kearshaw did then and there strike, stab, and penetrate, thereby giving unto herself, the said Hanna Kearshaw (so being lunatic and distracted) with the Razor aforesaid, in or upon the Throat or Gullet of her the said Hanna Kearshaw one Mortal Wound, of which said Mortal Wound, she the said Hanna Kearshaw then and there did die. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid, do say, that the said Hanna Kearshaw not being of sound Mind Memory, and Understanding but lunatic and distracted, in manner and by means aforesaid did kill herself. In Witness where of as well the said Coroner, as the said James Small  Foreman of the Said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and place above written.

Anthy Gell Coroner

Jas Small Foreman
