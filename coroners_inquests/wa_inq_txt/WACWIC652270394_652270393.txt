City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint Martin in the Fields Within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twelfth day of September in the Twenty seventh Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, Before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of  James Price then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Francis Cluley , James Hatch , James Stodart , John Sirman , Francis Nunn , John Lloyd , Michael Foreman , Samuel Holmes , William Gee , John Youngman , Joseph Wild , John Bradford , William Powell , James Wilson and John Havers good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said James Price came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said James Price on the said Twelfth day of September in the Year aforesaid being upon the Coal Barges on the River Thames at the Adelphi Wharf in the Parish aforesaid within the LIberty & County aforesaid And that in crossing the said Barges It so happened that the said James Price Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune fell into the said River Thames by means whereof he the said James Price in the Water of the said River was then and there suffocated and Drowned of which said Suffocation & Drowning he the said James Price then and there died And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said James Price in manner and by the Means aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his Death & not otherwise

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Francis Chiley  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Fans. Chiley Foreman
