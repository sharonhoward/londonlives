City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit. An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventeenth day of February in the Thirteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Milbourn then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Daniel Luckhurst , Joseph Higgs , William Wiggen , John Cassey , Henry Barr , John Delles , Daniel Tibson , Benjamin Viall , George Hassell , Edward Chandler , Thomas Gibson and Francis Casaude good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Milbourn came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said John Milbourn on the Sixteenth day of February in the Year aforesaid was found drowned and Suffocated in a certain Pond in Hyde Park in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That no Marks of violence appeared on the Body of the said John Milbourn , but how or by what Mans he became Drowned, no Evidence thereof doth appear to the Jurors. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Daniel Luckhurst Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard  Coroner
