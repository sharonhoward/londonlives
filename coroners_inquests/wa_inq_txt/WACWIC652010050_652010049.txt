Westminster City and Liberty in the County of Middlesex } to wit An Inquisition Indented and taken at the Parish of St. George Hanover Square in the Liberty of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of Saint Peter at Westminster the Seventeenth day of Agust in the first Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the faith and so forth and in the Year of our Lord One Thousand seven hundred and sixty one Before John Feary  Coroner for our Sovereign Lord the King for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid, Upon a View of the Body of Christian Clipping then and there lying Dead by the Oaths of John Pinchbeck , William Jones , Richard Burton , John Keat , William Stanbank , Nicholas Welch , Preston Thomas Bartholomew , John Clare , John Taylor , James Sedgwick Moulins , and John Stonehouse , Good and Lawfull Men of the said City and Liberty, who being Sworn and Charged to Inquire for our Sovereign Lord the King how when where and in what manner the said Christian Clipping came to her Death say upon their Oaths. That on Friday the fourteenth day of August Instant, the said Christian Clipping not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and Distracted into a certain Ditch or Water leading to Ra gh in the Parish Liberty and County aforesaid did Voluntarily cast and throw herself by means of which Casting and Throwing she the said Christian Clipping was then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning she the said Christian Clipping then and there instantly Died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Christian Clipping in manner and by the means aforesaid not being of Sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but Lunatick and Distracted did Drown and kill herself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition set their hands and Seals the day Year and at the Place abovementioned

Jno. Feary Coroner
