City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Sixteenth day of June in the Eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Thomas Birch then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of John Hyett Thomas Mason , John Amor , Thomas Taylor , Andrew Everill , William Copcut , Charles King , Edward Pribble , Edward Ellis , John Towler , Samuel Sheen , John Tims , Rice Jones , Charles Davis , John Dilman Inglechart and Lewis Vaughan good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Thomas Birch came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said Thomas Birch on the Twelfth day of June in the Year aforesaid, going into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, there to Cath himself, It so happened that accidentally casually and by Misfortune he the said Thomas Birch was in the Waters of the said River, then and there suffocated and Drowned; of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said St Thomas Birch then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, that the said Thomas Birch in Manner and by the Means aforesaid accidentally casually and by Misfortune cause to his Death, and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Co Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition at there Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first above written

Tho. Prickard  Coroner .

John Hyett Foreman
