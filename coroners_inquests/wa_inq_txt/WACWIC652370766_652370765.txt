City and Liberty of Westminster , In the County of Middlesex ,} to wit,  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Dwelling House of Edward plank the Sign of the Ship in Swallow Street  Parish of St. George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirteenth day of December 1797 in the thirty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France, and Ireland King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Anthony Gell , Esq.  Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Mary Pearson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are here under written, and Seals affixed, good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Mary Pearson came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say that the said Mary Pearson on the eleventh day of December at the year aforesaid at the Parish & in the Liberty aforesaid. To Wit at the House of her the said Mary Pearson in Princes Street  Hanover Square died by the Visitation of God in a natural Way & not otherwise.

 IN WITNESS whereof, as well the said Coroner as the said Jurors, have to this Inqusition set their Hands and Seals the Day, Year, and Place first above written.

Anthy. Gell Coroner } Wm Booth Robt Marshall St Smith Thos Bowlker Phillip P

Henry Nichols Thomas Boone James Tidbury Jno Locke R. Banks John Gurney
