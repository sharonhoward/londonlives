City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at Knights bridge in the Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Fifteenth day of September in the Eighteenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of William Parslow then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Richard Beach , John Ashmore , Nathaniel Smith , Thomas Granger , John Read , Armech Winchley , Thomas Brasier , John Low , John Nichellow , Thomas Pugh James Macartney and James Carr , good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire fore our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said William Parslow came to h is Death, do upon their Oath say, That the said William Parslow on the fourteenth day of September in the Year aforesaid, going into the Serpentine River in Hyde Park at the parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, there to bath himself; It so happened that Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune he the said William Parslow was in the Water of the said River then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said William Parslow then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said William Parslow in Manner and by the Means aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune cam to his death, and not otherwise. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Richard Beach Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Richd: Beach Foreman
