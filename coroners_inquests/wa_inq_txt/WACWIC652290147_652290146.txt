City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Intended, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the thirteenth day of March in the twenty ninth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Elizabeth Swaby then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Thomas Stewart , George Jetterton , James Ball , Thomas Struttle, John White , Thomas Comerford , James Dunch , James Fryer , Joseph Munning , George Tomlinson , Charles Johnson , John Morris , George Eastwood , John George , Thomas Hinde , Andrew Maine and Stephen Smith good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Elizabeth Swaby came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Elizabeth Swaby not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and distracted on the Twelfth day of March in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid to Wit into the Bason in Hyde Park did cast and throw herself by means of which said casting and throwing she the said Elizabeth Swaby in the Water of the said Bason was then and there suffocated and Drowned of which said Suffocation and Drowning she the said Elizabeth Swaby then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that ther said Elizabeth Swaby not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but Lunatick and distracted in manner and by the Means aforesaid did Kill herself

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Thomas Stewart Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Thos Stewart Foreman
