City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex ,} to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign, taken for our sovereign Lord the King, at of St Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of Collegiate Church of st. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eleventh day of Novr 1793 in the Thirty fourth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain,France and Irela,d King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Hayworth then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of the several Jurors whose Names are behinder written and Seals Field

 Good and lawful Men of the said Liberty. duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enqure for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Haywood came to his Death, do upon their Oath say, that the said John Haywood on the said Eleventh day of November in the Year aforesaid at the County and in the Liberty aforesaid To Wit at the House of Mr Taylor Scotland and there being at the top of the said house employed as a Briklayer in the repairs thereof It so happened that iam lentally casually and by Misfor tune the the said John Haywood from the said House unto the Ground did fal. by means whereof he the said John Haywood did then and there receme divas mortal wounds on the Head & Body of the said John Haywood of which said Mortal Wounds be the said John Haywood then and there instantly died. And so the Jurors aforesiad upon their Oath aforesaid do say tat the said John Haywood in Manner and by the Means aforesaid accidentally casually and by Misfortune came to his death and not otherwise. In Witness where of as well the said coron as the said Juors have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the day Year & Place first above written

Anthy Gell Coroner

Smith Thos Shepley Joseph Fixen

Wm Stevent Jno Thos Winturell Theodore Freherme Wm Iles Thos
