City and Liberty of Westminster , in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at The Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Eleventh day of December in the twenty eighth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard  Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Hannah Rymer then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of William Vincent , Francis Ridley  William King William Taylor , John Brown , Allen North , John Peters , John Richer , George Palmer , John Mackingtosh , Thomas Bowhannan & Richard Hatch good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Elizabeth Rymer came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Hannah Rymer (suspected to have been murdered) on the Eighth day of December in the Year aforesaid at the said Parish of Saint Margaret within the Liberty and County aforesaid departed this Life by the Visitation of God in a natural Way and not otherwise.

In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said William Vincent  Foreman of the said Jurors on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

William Vincent Foreman
