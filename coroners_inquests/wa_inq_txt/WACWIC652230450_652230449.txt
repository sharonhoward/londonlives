City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Seventeenth day of September in the twenty third Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of a new born Female Child then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Joseph Jarvis  John Mitchell  Adam Ragg , Thomas Lowthorp , William Smith , George Lewis , Thomas Bandy , Edward Cooke , William Lloyd , John Spearing , Samuel Shepherd Robert Nicolls and Richard Reader good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Female Child came to h er Death, do upon their Oath say That on the Fifteenth day of September in the Year aforesaid at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County, aforesaid a certain Woman to the Jurors aforesaid unknown did bear, and bring forth the said Female Child alive And that the said Woman unknown not having the fear of God before her Eyes but being moved and seduced by the Instigation of the Devil and wicked by devising and intending to Kill and Murder the said Female Child on the Day and in the Year aforesaid with Force and Arms at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid in and upon the said Female Child then and there being alive and in the Peace of God and our said Lord the King then and there being feloniously wilfully and of her Malice fore thought did make an Assault, And that the said Woman unknown then and there feloniously wilfully and of her Malice fore thought (with Intent and Design that the said Female Child might bleed to Death) did refuse neglect and omit to tye and cause to be tyed the Navel String of the said Female Child By Means whereof the said Female Child did then and there violently bleed through the said Navel String of the said Female Child And the said Female Child of the great Effusion and Loss of Blood through the said Navel String did then and there languish and languishing did live on which same Day and Year last mentioned the said Female Child at the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid by reason of the great Effusion and Loss of Blood aforesaid did die. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said Woman unknown her the said Female Child in Manner and Form aforesaid feloniously wilfully and of her Malice fore thought did Kill and Murder against the Peace of our said Lord the King his Crown and Dignity In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner, as the said Joseph Jarvis  Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his Fellows, in their presence have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals the Day Year and Place abovementioned

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Jos Jarvis Foreman}
