City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex } to wit.   An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint George Hanover Square within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Peter, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twentieth day of May in the Seventeenth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman , Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of Mary Salmon an Infant then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of Robert Sayer , John Pendre , John Saunders , Henry Akin , Joseph Williams , John Baber , William Knight , Peter Paul , Samuel Powell , Thomas Hopkins . James Blair and Richard Breach good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said Mary Salmon came to her Death, do upon their Oath say That the said Mary Salmon the Infant to Wit, of the Age of two Years, on the Nineteenth day of May in the Year aforesaid being alone in the Lodging Room or Apartment of Mary Salmon her Mother in the Dwelling House of Thomas Hopkins situate in Down Street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid It so happened that the said Mary Solman the Infant then and there Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune fell into a certain Washing Tub upon the Floor in the said Apartment, and in the Water in the said Tub was then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning she the said Mary Salmon then and there died. And so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say, That the said Mary Salmon in Manner and by the Means aforesaid Accidentally Casually and by Misfortune came to his Death. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said Robert Sayer Foreman of the said Jurors, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day, Year & Place first above written

Tho. Prickard Coroner

Robert Sayer Foreman
