City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex to wit. 

An Inquisition Indented taken for Our Sovereign Lord the King at the Parish of saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate church of St. Peter Westminster in the County of Middlesex , the Sixth day of December in the Fifth Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Thirdby the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the Faith and so forth before Thomas Prichard Gentleman Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty On View of the Body of John Brett suspected to have been Murdered, then and there lying Deced upon the Oath of Joseph Jarvis  Francis Aday  William Perry , Thomas Douglass , Elisha Dobrey , James Maund , Edward Haines , Richard Collins ,. William Broughton , Gabriel Smith , Adam Nelsom  John Williams , John Hill  George Garrard Thomas Barinton  Joseph Prosser , & Richard Badham Good and Lawful Men of the said Liberty duly Chosen who. being then and there duly Sworn and Charged to inquire for our said Lord the King when her and by what Means, the said John Brett came to his Death,do up on their Oathsay, that on the Fifth day of December in the Year aforesaid, the said John Brett a Cobber, was found Dead in his Working Stall or Shop. situate and being in a certain Street called Cross street in the Parish aforesaid within the Liberty and County aforesaid, That the said John Brett died by a fit of an apoplexy or other sudden Visitation of god, and not by any Hurt or Injury received from any Person or Persons whatsoever to the Knowledge of the said Jurors No Marks of Violence appearing on the Body of the said John Brett . In Witness where of as well the said Coroner, as the said Joseph Jarvis Foreman of the said Jurors, on the behalf of himself and the rest of his said Fellows, in their presence, have to this Inquisition set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place first above Written

Tho.Prickard Coroner

Jos Jarvis Foreman
