City and Liberty of Westminster in the County of Middlesex .} to wit.  An Inquisition Indented, taken for our Sovereign Lord the King, at the Parish of Saint James within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of St. Pater, Westminster , in the County of Middlesex , the Twenty fifth day of October in the Twenty first Year of the Regin of our Sovereign Lord GEORGE the Third, by the Grace of God, of Great-Britain, France and Ireland, King, Defender of the Faith, and so forth, before Thomas Prickard , Gentleman, Coroner of our said Lord the King for the said City and Liberty, on View of the Body of John Pattinson then and there lying dead, upon the Oath of James Filewood , John Williams , John Gladwell , William Frisby , Robert Clarke , Richard Williams , John Nimms , Edward Bayley , Richard Payne , John Jarvis , William Clements , James Elding John Lisk , John Bland , Richard Sims , John Aylesbury  James Lock Richard Savage  George Ballard , & Thomas Andrews good and lawful Men of the said Liberty, duly chosen, who being then and there duly sworn and charged to enquire for our said Lord the King, when, how, and by what Means the said John Pattinson came to h is Death do upon their Oath say That the said John Pattinson , not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding, but lunatick and distracted, on the Twenty fourth day of October in the Year aforesaid at the Parish and in the Liberty and County aforesaid a certain Pistol charged with Gunpowder and a leaden Bullet which he the said John Pattinson then and there had and held in his Right Hand, into the Mouth of him the said John Pattinson did then and there shoot off and discharge, by Means whereof he the said John Pattinson did then and there give unto himself with the leaden Bullet aforesaid, so discharged and shot out of the Pistol aforesaid by the force of the Gunpowder aforesaid, in the Head of him the said John Pattinson one mortal Wound, of which said mortal Wound he the said John Pattinson then and there instantly died. and so the Jurors aforesaid upon their Oath aforesaid do say that the said John Pattinson not being of sound Mind Memory and Understanding but lunatick and distracted in manner and by the means aforesaid did kill himself. In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the said James Filewood Foreman of the said Jurors to this Inquisition have set their Hands and Seals, the Day Year and Place abovewritten

Tho Prickard Coroner James Filewood Foreman
