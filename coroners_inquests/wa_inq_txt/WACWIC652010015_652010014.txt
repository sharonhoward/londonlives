Westminster City and Liberty in the County of Middlesex } to wit

An Inquisition Indented and taken at the Parish of St. John the Evangelist in the City of Westminster within the Liberty of the Dean and Chapter of the Collegiate Church of Saint Peter at Westminster the Twenty fourth Day of February in the First Year of the Reign of our Sovereign Lord George the Third by the Grace of God of Great Britain France and Ireland King Defender of the Faith and so forth and in the Year of our Lord One Thousand seven hundred and sixty one Before John Feary Coroner for our Sovereign Lord the King for the City and Liberty of Westminster aforesaid, upon a Veiw of the Body of John Stone then and there lying Dead by the Oaths of Thomas Lloyd , Willim Hepworth , Matthew Pope , Kindley Carter Joseph Gaunt , Francis Hornsby , John Hallew , John Sugg , Robert Murray , John Chewter , David Murray , John Jacob Richard Virgo , Thomas Barnes , and Henry Foley Good and Lawfull Men of the said City and Liberty who being Sworn and Charged to Inquire for our Sovereign Lord the King how when where and in what manner the said John Stone come to his Death say upon their Oaths That on Saturday the twenty frist day of February instant the said John Stone being in a Boat in the River of Thames in Company with one Alexander Hebble , and the said Boat being laden with Garden Box and being coming down the said Riven to the Horseferry it so happened that in their Passage a sudden squall of Wind arose, and the said Boat filled with Water, and instantly Junk by means whereof the said John Stone in the Waters thereof way then and there Suffocated and Drowned, of which said Suffocation and Drowning he the said John Stone then and there instantly Died And so the said Jurors aforesaid upon their Oaths aforesaid do say, that the said John Stone in manner and by the means aforesaid accidentally casually and by Misfortune came to his Death, and not otherwise And that the said Boat was the cause of the Death of the said John Stone , and is of the value of Two Shillings and six pence, and the property and in the Possession of John Berwick of Tothil fields Westminster Gardner, the Master of the said John Stone , or his Assigns In Witness whereof as well the said Coroner as the Foreman of the Jurors aforesaid have to this Inquisition put their hands and Seals the day, Year, and at the place abovesaid.

Jno. Feary  Coroner .

Thos: Lloyd Foreman
